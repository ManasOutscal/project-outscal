We work hand in hand with Sumo Digital’s UK studios to build ground-breaking games and content for some of the world’s biggest publishers including Microsoft, Sony, Apple and Sega. It’s an exciting time to be part of Sumo, winners of the Develop: Star Awards 2019 Best Studio; big things are on the horizon and we want you to be part of our continued journey. Join us! Within this role you will: Work with the lead artist to develop, explore and style the games user interface and develop icons, buttons, frames and panels etc. Create user interface concepts and motion graphics to demonstrate transitions and interactions as well as execute them within the game engine. Explore and assemble visual material to define and improve the visual style of the game. Solve problems effectively and creatively with an ability to scale quality when time or deadlines require it. Collaborate closely with UX designer to iterate and consider the gameplay implications of the design and the overall player experience. Detach yourself from personal tastes to approach and problem solve from the players perspective. Always thinking about how we can be better. We need you to have: Diverse UI style portfolio from casual friendly games through to mature titles. (Experience in the Adobe graphics suite essential) Great illustration skills to develop the UI from freehand sketch to accurate vectors. Clear consideration of colour theory, shape, scale, form, silhouette. Basic understanding of 3D packages and using them as part of a concept workflow. Can do attitude with confidence in your ability to work to a design spec / brief whilst able to use your own intuition to fill in the gaps. Fastidious attention to detail in order to push the visual and technical barriers further. Knowledge of game engine UI systems such as UGUI as bonus. An open approach to collaboration and feedback from others. Excellent communication skills never afraid to ask questions. What we offer: Our Studio is unique in India, giving our staff the opportunity to work directly on all leading gaming platforms, at every stage of game development. Being part of Sumo's Pune studio is a rewarding experience, one that requires dedication, a strong sense of ownership over your work and an overwhelming enthusiasm for the games you work on. In return we offer competitive salary, flexi-time & benefits, and excellent working environment and most importantly we will give you the room to be yourself and live your passion. Additionally, we provide relocation reimbursement from other cities, a performance-linked bonus in addition to the fixed salary, opportunities to attend industry conferences and a complete and all-encompassing medical insurance package for our employees and their children. Come join the team.

Senior Game Designer in Ubisoft

Requires Skils: OOPS

Jobs available in Pune

Job Description About the Organization Ubisoft is a leading developer and publisher of video games worldwide whose brand portfolio covers blockbusters such as Assassin’s Creed, Watch Dogs, The Division, Prince of Persia and Splinter Cell, Ghost Recon, Rainbow Six, Rayman, Just Dance as well as games for the whole family, from Imagine and Petz to Raving Rabbids. To continue building on its achievements for the future, Ubisoft is looking for new talent for its growing Indian studio in Pune! We favor diversity, creativity, drive and team spirit. If you have the skills and the desire to succeed, we want you to be a part of this exciting period of growth. JOB DESCRIPTION We are looking for a Senior Game Designer to join the Ubisoft Pune studio as part of the well established Just Dance Now mobile production & live-ops team. Senior Game Designer is a high-level position where you are expected to be the evangelist and core voice of Just Dance Now, be able to guide others, manage yourself, have a certain level of autonomy, and take responsibility for yourself and the others. You will lead the Just Dance Now mobile team of developers and artists while working with production, live-ops, analytics and marketing teams to deliver highly engaging features and updates to the game. Maintaining and improving the Quality of Service is the key element for Just Dance Now. This is a permanent position based in Pune, India, with the opportunity to work partially from home according to our Flexible Workplace Policy. You will have the following responsibilities on Just Dance Now: Guide the execution of the existing roadmap. Work with the Producer and other key stakeholders to establish the future roadmap. Own and drive the quality of the player experience by providing creative design solutions that are in line with the Just Dance Now vision. Create and playtest UX prototypes to help improve the player experience. Design new progression and reward systems to improve the user engagement. Work with the marketing and live-ops teams to guide and evaluate the creation of the marketing, promotional, and event-based assets and other communication material. Communicate your designs clearly and be able to persuade others about an idea or vision. Create documents, presentations, wireframes, user flow, user stories, etc. that are clear and concise. Be open and constructive about any feedback or inputs received from others in the team. Ability to pivot and evolve ideas, if required, to match new direction and feedback. Collaborate and work closely with the rest of the team to ensure that the design specifications are achievable within the technical and production constraints. Be willing to identify and push the constraints where applicable and feasible. Follow-up on features post launch to improve, evolve or trim any components based on user feedback and user data. REQUIREMENTS A strong interest in mobile games. 5+ years of Game Design experience in mobile free-to-play games with atleast 2 successfully shipped titles. Experience designing features and content for live-ops on at least 1 mobile free-to-play title. Experience mentoring junior colleagues and driving multi-disciplinary teams in a design leadership role. Good grasp of prototyping tools such as Adobe XD and Photoshop. A keen eye for quality and design excellence. Strong ability to deconstruct high level systems and product direction into specific technical details, new features, and tasks. Ability to work in a highly collaborative, interdisciplinary, iterative environment, while delivering within deadlines and time constraints. Ability to prioritize and self-manage. A great listener with superb written and oral communication skills in English. ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS (nice to have) Knowledge of collaboration tools like Confluence, Jira and Miro. Experience working with Unity 3D. Prior experience playing Just Dance titles. Optimism, humor, open mindedness, with a can-do attitude and a desire to constantly improve.
