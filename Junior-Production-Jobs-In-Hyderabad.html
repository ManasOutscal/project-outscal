EA Sports Mobile is looking for an Game Product Manager. Consumer Internet, Mobile Product Development and Digital Retail would be other close domain matches and we look for demonstrable depth in providing Strategic and Functional leadership. Key attributes include authentic understanding of Digital Consumer Behaviours and Business models, Sharp analytical skills, Ability to create and institutionalize Product and Business measurement frameworks and Articulate strategies. Ability to communicate in a global environment and the temperament to perform in a truly Cross-Functional set-up is critical. Help us overcome technical hurdles to bring new, innovative ad products to market at scale Player behavior: Understanding the players’ motivation is key to design valuable tests and interpret results SQL: The ability to write complex SQL queries to analyze our databases Analytical coding: Using tools such as R, Python (and also Excel) for analytical purposes Qualifications: You have worked extensively (1-3 years) as a Product Manager in Mobile Advertising domain. Expertise in analyzing requirements, creating roadmaps for technical products to maximize advertising revenues. Experience in mobile gaming is a plus. Proven expertise in writing and executing complex SQL Queries, working with large databases (telemetry) to figure out actionable insights, ability to get your hands dirty with R and/or Python, Tableau, and Excel as needed to analyze and report out metrics. Ability to navigate through complex dashboards of ad services providers such as Google, Facebook, Ironsource, etc. to figure out the right focus areas for the team.
