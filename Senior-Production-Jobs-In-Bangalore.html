Lead Product Manager in Gameberry Labs

Requires Skils: OOPS

Jobs available in Bangalore Urban

About Gameberry Labs Gameberry Labs was founded in 2017 with a mission to Build games that players love to grow old with. Our games, Ludo Star and Parchisi star, have gone on to become viral hits across the globe. Today, Gameberry Labs is one of India's most successful and profitable gaming companies, with revenues exceeding $25M(Rs. 190cr) in 2020 and $40M(Rs. 300cr) in 2021 And we believe we are just getting started. Our goal is to become one of the World's best gaming studios In the next 2-3 years, we want to grow 5-6 times by building games that our players deeply love. Our Games Our flagship games, Ludo Star and Parchisi Star, have more than 150 Million+ downloads globally, and about 4 million Daily Active Users. Parchisi Star is the #1 Parchisi game globally, with a very big fan following in Spain and Latin America. Parchisi Star has recently crossed 100M downloads on the Play Store. Ludo Star is the leading Ludo game in the Middle East & Pakistan, and 2nd biggest Ludo game in the world by revenue We are also in the early stage development of multiple new games across different genres. Stay tuned for more updates GBL is looking for a motivated and passionate Lead Product Manager who can help manage and direct the performance of our Top Games. Responsibilities: Responsible for the full product/game lifecycle, from writing specifications, working across multi-disciplinary teams: design, art, and engineering to launch core features within our top games. Exhibit sound product judgment, ability to formulate product strategy and present clear measurable objectives that will lead to achieving our business goals. Mentoring & Guiding PMs & Sr PMs. Requirements: 7+ years of experience in Product Management in consumer web or game development is preferred but not required. Ability to create wire-frames and product specifications. Strong project management skills and ability to present work to executive staff. Good Data Analysis Skills. Passion for Games and Creating fun user experiences. Attention to detail & Bias for Action. Extremely important to be a Self Starter, Driven person who loves solving unsolved problems. Previous start-up, social networking and/or consulting experience is a strong plus.
