Job Description

IllFonic is looking for a Senior Technical Artist. This role requires an experienced, technically minded individual that has previous tech-art exposure. The responsibilities include creating tools, shaders and scripts while adhering to schedules and project goals. If this is you, then apply today to join this innovative game studio where you will help build visually compelling gameplay environments. About the Company Founded in 2007, IllFonic is an independent video game developer and publisher with studios in Lakewood, Colorado, Tacoma, Washington, and Austin, Texas. Our staff is composed of video game, music, and technology veterans redefining how indie and middle-market games are made and marketed. Current and Past titles / collaborations include Ghostbusters: Spirits Unleashed, Arcadegeddon, Predator: Hunting Grounds, Friday the 13th: The Game, Dead Alliance, Star Citizen, Evolve, Armored Warfare, and Nexuiz. Responsibilities Working with Producers to estimate the duration of tech art tasks, and then enforcing deliverables and standards Propose solutions and optimize content for memory and performance concerns on PC and Consoles Develop and maintain art asset pipelines, and collaborate with the art team to optimize workflows, authoring new tools as necessary Be proactive, develop and implement new ideas/solutions to problems as they arise Advise on emerging graphics technology while assessing its usefulness within current pipeline and schedules Assist in various areas of game art production to help assure the timely delivery of the product Requirements Portfolio of work demonstrating strong proficiency as a Technical Artist 4 or more years of professional experience in the video game industry with at least 2 shipped titles Solid understanding of artist workflows and toolsets Proficient in C++, along with Python / PySide and MEL with knowledge of 2d/3d art packages (in-game development support) Familiar with UE4 blueprints or equivalent visual scripting Ability to write clean, maintainable and extensible code with attention to reusability Optimizing Art Assets (Environment, Character, Props, VFX and Rigs) Ability to create simple and complex shader graphs, as well as the optimization of those shaders Tool support for animation tasks, animation rigging and skinning tasks Proactive and solution orientated Well-developed troubleshooting skills - must able to quickly identify and resolve art issues and demonstrate versatility Strong communication skills, both written and verbal A customer first mentality A passion for games Benefits & Compensation Flexible work location options including remote, hybrid or in office 100% employee paid medical, dental and vision coverage + 50% coverage for dependents 401k + 4% company match Competitive salary in the independent game developer market, with career progression and continued education encouraged and supported by management at all levels Royalty Bonus Plan paid out after each calendar quarter during the year Wellness Program that rewards work-life balance Unlimited PTO and almost 7 weeks of mandated time off of work annually including First Friday Wellness Days of each month, Summer Break the week of 4th of July, Winter Break the last two weeks of the year, and an additional 7 holidays during the year Moonlighting allowed for all non-competitive endeavors
