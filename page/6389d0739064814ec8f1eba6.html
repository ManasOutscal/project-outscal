Job Description

Crowdform is a digital product, game and venture studio in London + São Paulo. We build digital products and launch ventures for the next evolution of the web. A global team of designers, engineers and technologists, we launch ventures from scratch and partner with companies we believe in to create products and games that transform and endure. Backed by a network that’s created over $2b in enterprise value, we’re building the next generation of products for the decentralised web with a focus on innovations in gaming and DeFi. We’re growing quickly and are looking for ambitious people who share our passion for technology, design and improving people’s lives using digital products. We have a challenge for those who: wants to explore ideas to refine our product concept, discovering new ways to use technology strategically; build projects to design, develop and launch fully-functional digital products; love all digital design things and be comfortable working in a fast-paced studio environment; likes to work to always do better, giving post-launch support to iterate and improve our products for our customers. Responsabilities: Participate in the complete cycle of product development from analysis, prototyping and testing with users to the continuous improvement of solutions. Create game specifications and designing expansion packs, apart from contributing to the overall game concept and storyline. Code the base engine of the game, generate game scripts and storyboards, developing game milestones and checkpoints. Meet with the design team to discuss game concepts and translate design ideas into a playable game code. Contribute to audio and animation design and animate characters and creating levels. Identify and communicate best practices for Game engineering, participating in design and code review, besides working with troubleshoot code and fixing bugs. Using application program interfaces (APIs) – a set of pre-built commands that allow different software to interact. Programming artificial intelligence for non-player characters within the game Skills required Have a background in software engineering or related careers. Experience with full-cycle game design. Experience building libraries and APIs. Strong C# skills Have worked in Frontend/Backend/Game Development in high performance environments. Knowledge of the latest gaming trends. Strong artistic and technical skills. Ability to work as part of a team. Strong communication skills to understand clients needs, communicate progress, manage feedback and support developer implementation. Wide-ranging knowledge of gaming trends 6+ years of work experience as a game developer (2D and 3D) 3+ years of experience with Unity, with good understanding of Animations, UI effects, and Audio. Experience publishing games to Mobile and Desktop. Experience building Multiplayer games. Experience with Web Development technologies a plus (Node, React). Knowledge of Rest API. Experience with other Game Engines and Frameworks a plus. Experience with Graphics and Audio software applications a plus.
