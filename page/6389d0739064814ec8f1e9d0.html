Job Description

Unknown Worlds is seeking an accomplished 3D environment artist to work on a new unannounced franchise. Our ideal candidate will possess strong sculpting skills and have a passion for creating and texturing natural environments and vegetation. They will have experience with end-to-end art pipelines - working from initial concept drawings through modeling, texturing, polish and optimization. This is a remote position on a fully remote team. RESPONSIBILITIES Collaborate closely with the concept team and other environment artists to create stunning, expressive 3D environments and bring our game worlds to life Work with the art team to refine and improve our art pipelines and workflows Work with graphics programmers to develop new systems and techniques and help manage texture and shader efficiency Assist with integration of art assets into the game QUALIFICATIONS 3+ years’ experience as a 3D artist in the games industry with minimum one shipped title High level of proficiency with Max, Maya or Blender, Z-Brush, and Substance Designer & Painter Skilled at texturing and developing materials in Substance Designer Skilled at sculpting natural environments and vegetation Strong written and verbal communication Strong traditional drawing/painting or sculpting skills Self-starting, organized and highly motivated Desire to contribute creatively to the development of our games outside of art PREFERENCES Experience with Unity recommended Experience with optimizing art for mobile and/or console Experience with other art subdisciplines a plus (animation, rigging, VFX) Comfortable working with a completely remote team We want to make games for the world.
