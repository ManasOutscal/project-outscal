Job Description

As a Monetization Designer, you are responsible for the overall monetization plan across multiple games. You will design monetization features and progression systems and build the roadmap for live service content. You are the point of contact for all monetization topics. You will collaborate with studios and publishing to ensure visibility of monetization KPIs and ensure that the monetization strategy is competently executed. In this position, you will: Design monetization and progression systems for several different game genres (racing, sports, FPS). Build live service roadmap of content and events. Monitor key monetization KPIs and deliver regular reports and recommendations to the game team and Zordix leadership. Identify monetization problems and create solutions in collaboration with the live game team. Analyze market trends and monetization best practices and communicate strategic recommendations. Ideally, you have: A minimum of 3 years’ experience in the video game industry as a designer, product manager, or equivalent experience. A minimum of 1 shipped title. Experience with data analysis, statistics, and forecasting. Experience with F2P game, including monetization metrics and systems like Battle Passes.
