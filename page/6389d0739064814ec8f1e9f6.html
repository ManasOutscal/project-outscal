Job Description

Osmo is seeking an FX Artist and Unity Generalist to help us create and implement content inside our Unity Projects - AR and Mixed Reality products for kids. Must be comfortable with ambiguity, fast prototyping, integration and testing. Responsibilities Create, update and polish game assets inside Unity (focus on FX particle systems) Use our Timeline tool to adjust and polish the timing of assets based on gameplay, storytelling and narratives: Animation, VO, Sound FX, VFX, Etc.. Collaborate with a cross functional team of designers, artists, marketers, and engineers on everything from games for kids to the eCommerce experience for parents of art assets within the Game Development pipeline. Test art assets as they are delivered for integration and provide the solutions for any fixes. Requirements BFA/ BS/ MS degree and/or experience in games-related or creative field A VFX portfolio showcasing examples of work completed inside Unity Engine Strong understanding of Unity and creating particle systems, integrating UI assets and prefabs Solid understanding of the concepts of art content creation, asset pipelines and git revision control Good artistic and technical eye ensuring all visuals look and behave according to Art standards and enhance gameplay Solid problem solving and time management skills Great communicator with strong verbal and written skills Flexible and enthusiastic about learning new things Nice to have Familiarity with Spine both rigging and animation Understanding of C# and git Experience designing applications for a global and multilingual user (kids) base Experience working with AI, AR, and VR technologies
