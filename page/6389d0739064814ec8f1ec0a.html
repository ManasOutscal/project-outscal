Job Description

Want to be onboard a rocketship and help build a new and groundbreaking MMORPG called Ember Sword? Frontend Web Developer As a Frontend Web Developer at Bright Star Studios Web team, you'll be part of the core group of developers that takes care of everything outside of the game like homepage, bots, blockchain, APIs, and so on. Your main responsibility is to maintain and build the frontend to our website with an ever growing number of features. If you are looking to have an impact on an ambitious MMORPG, then join us and pioneer solutions that will help us scale to the next level. We are expanding with rocket speed, while being on our quest to make the next big MMORPG. Generally, building something from scratch should excite you rather than terrify you. Don't worry, you won't be doing this alone; throughout the last year our team grew by 125% and we have various backgrounds from companies such as Riot Games, Blizzard, Apple, Unity, Lucas Art, and more. We have plenty of ideas and we are looking for more talented people to help us fulfill them. Responsibilities: Maintaining our website Creating new web components and pages Integration with game features Integration via web3 with Immutable X (blockchain) Maintain and develop new features for our Discord bots About Bright Star Studios We are crazy ambitious. That’s one of the reasons why Ember Sword is created on our own custom game engine. The game should be playable via a browser and it is a matter of seconds from you entering our site until you are ingame. If you want to play in another region, you don’t need to start from scratch. You’ll simply have all your gear, levels, and items with you - This means you can always play with your friends, regardless of what server you both started on. No more annoying scenarios where you have to make new characters or pay to move to another server. All your skins are owned by the players, not the gaming companies. All cosmetics are dropped in-game as free NFT’s, allowing the player to truly own it, and enables them to legally trade assets outside the game. Cosmetics have zero in-game advantages and you can not pay to win. It’s free to play and it’s a game by gamers, to gamers. We are able to proudly state that, as our team consists of former professional Counter-Strike and World of Warcraft players. Want to know more? Everyone is invited Because together we are better. It's our policy to provide equal employment opportunities for all applicants and members of Bright Star Studios. We know that fresh and varied perspectives will make us better at what we do, so however you identify and whatever background you bring with you, we're excited to hear from you. Don't be discouraged if you feel you don't meet every one of the requirements for a role, there's always room for growth at Bright Star Studios. You can work remote or join us in one of our offices located in Denmark, Netherlands, Serbia or Romania. We can even assist with relocation if you please. Listen to our podcast to know more about us, we recommend "Working at Bright Star Studios" and "Meet The Team", to understand more about our culture If you spot a role to make you want to jump out of bed in the morning, we are waiting to hear from you! Requirements The MUSTs: Expertise using HTML/CSS/JavaScript Years of hands-on experience with React.js Experience using NPM and Node.js Experience building responsive web apps A good understanding of design patterns and UX From Denmark, or willing to relocate The GOOD TO HAVEs: Genuine love for MMOs and/or RPGs Experience with TypeScript Experience with NFT’s and blockchain (Web3) Understanding of WebGL and web animations Experience using Docker and/or CI/CD Understand the principles of SEO Experience with graphic design applications Knowledge of composition of SVGs and graphical elements Benefits We offer remote options and flexible working hours. We offer balance between work and personal life and are encouraged with open paid time off. All employees getting onboard 2022 will get a token package, and therefore have an incentive to make the best MMORPG. Besides that, we have the usual: Free coffee and tea. Social gatherings. Board game nights. Free office snacks and soft drinks. Friday bars. Health insurance.
