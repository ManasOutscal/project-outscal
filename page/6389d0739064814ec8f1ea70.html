Job Description

Team17 is establishing an ambitious and exciting new stand-alone virtual studio dedicated to the further development and on-going support of Hell Let Loose. Hell Let Loose is a WW2 platoon-based realistic multiplayer FPS videogame for PC and consoles set during the Second World War. With over 6 million players already, this is just the start for us! Team 17 is on the lookout for highly talented Level Designer who can bring their skills and creativity to this exciting and successful franchise. You will have a background in level design, working on both the ideation and construction of game worlds of at least one published title under your belt. You must have a keen grasp of what it takes to make a good level, and a full understanding of the theory that underpins the players’ journey through your level. This is a highly collaborative role, where you will be operating proactively alongside a mixed team of coders, artists, and game designers. You have the technical and communication skills to communicate your ideas with your peers and the wider team. The things you will do… Defining the level content either visually, verbally or in written form. Implementing or assisting in the implementation of that content, and to ensure the content is of high quality. Working proactively with the other members of the team, including the directors and stakeholders, to ensure that the design-related goals of the project are met in a timely manner. Ensuring a high quality of work on their assigned project, primarily from a level design point of view. The communication and championing of game content, concept and vision to artists, programmers, producers, marketing staff and all others involved in the development process. Performing research and understanding real world WW2 battlefields. How will you do it… Strong working knowledge of Unreal Engine 4. Communicate a level’s vision or elements; to designers, artists, programmers, producers, marketing staff, and others involved in the development process. Previous professional experience in a level design role, with at least one published title. Understanding of crafting gameplay experiences from concept to completion Knowledge of 3D modelling packages for white boxing Experience of level editing tools, including a strong demonstratable knowledge of the Unreal Engine 4. Prior experience using node-based visual script logic and the creation of scripted events Knowledge of agile processes and project tracking software Familiarity with rapid prototyping. Your Benefits… Annual Bonus, Pension matched up to 8%, Share match Scheme, £1000 of free shares for all new colleagues. BUPA private medical insurance including cover for pre-existing conditions and BUPA Dental Work Anywhere. Whilst our studio is virtual you have access to utilise our three UK offices. Team17 is an equal opportunities employer with a commitment to building a diverse workforce, representative of the world we live and game in! Accessibility is at the heart of our games, and we reflect that in how we operate as a business. Should you require adjustments or flexible arrangements to interview, or take up a role at Team17, please let us know in the notes section of your application and we will accommodate to the best of our ability
