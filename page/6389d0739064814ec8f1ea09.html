Job Description

Title: Lead VFX Artist Location: Anywhere in N & S America, Europe The ideal candidate in this role is an experienced VFX Artist in games, with a balance of technical problem solving and artistic skill and a passion for all manner of VFX, from gameplay and combat driven systems, to environmental atmospherics and destruction. With a highly collaborative development culture at PTW/1518, experience at effectively working and communicating alongside a diverse team, across all departments and specialization, to solve ambitious visual, gameplay, and technical problems together, is extremely important for success in this role In This Role You will design and implement high-quality AAA VFX to support gameplay and art direction. Create and optimize VFX to run within performance limitations. Work closely with art, animation, design, and programming team to anticipate and solve challenges within given constraints and visual requirements. Translate references, concept art, game design briefs, and verbal directions into prototypes and implement real-time VFX in-game. We are looking for Mid to senior level of professional VFX experience in the game industry and you must have a strong reel/portfolio showing executed real-time FX work with a minimum of one shipped AAA game. What you’ll be doing Creating amazing looking real time FX with Unity. Collaborate with the Art, Gameplay, Design, and Animation teams to develop in-game FX elements, from early look development and prototyping to final polished implementation. Use our engine authoring toolset, Maya, Houdini, Substance, and various other supplemental FX authoring packages to create stunning VFX particles, systems, textures, and simulations. Directly implement VFX content, including in-world placement and scripting of systemic/gameplay driven behaviours Contribute to the development and enhancement of our proprietary engine VFX systems, including particle effect authoring and scripting and sequencing tools and pipelines. Placing and triggering FX within the world. Working with the Art, Design and Engineering teams to find solutions to artistic challenges. Develop a deep understanding of the art direction and create effects to support the vision. Create effects to realistic performance budgets and take ownership of the memory and performance costs of your effects. Requirements What we’re looking for Experience of creating Realtime particle effects in Unity Experience of using industry-standard content creation software (e.g. Maya, 3ds Max, Photoshop, Substance) to create assets to use in-engine. Experience as a VFX artist in games, using a real-time authoring toolset (Unity) Experience with various supplemental 3D and VFX authoring packages (Maya, Houdini, Substance, etc.) Portfolio that demonstrates a breadth of both technical and artistic execution of real-time VFX Interest for games and the future of Virtual Reality Experience with shaders. Strong Unity skills and experience with other appropriate features: Knowledge of game production pipeline and life cycle. A critical eye for the timing, animation, and motion of real-world objects and visual FX Ability to create all art needed for the FX from prototype to final FX Desire and knowledge of how to push our effects, editing tools, and pipeline further Experience with scripting in Blueprint, Python, etc is a plus Abilities in secondary areas such as modelling, skinning, animation, or lighting Experience working with multiple platforms memory limitations Experience with real-time networked multiplayer games Passionate about games and art in games.
