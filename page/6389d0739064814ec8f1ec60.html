Job Description

About PlaytestCloud PlaytestCloud and our products power the player-centric development of modern mobile games. Over 60% of the studios listed in the Top 100 grossing games charts on the App Store have tested their games with PlaytestCloud, so we have a lot of experience under our belt. Our aim is to make it 100% by the end of 2022! We’re the premier user testing provider for mobile games - we help game studios worldwide make the best games they possibly can. Our online platform enables testing iOS and Android games with real players around the world and provides in-game video recordings, surveys, and reports which game studios use to craft amazing player experiences. We’re bootstrapped and profitable, with 300+ paying game studios and 1,000,000+ playtesters. At PlaytestCloud, everybody contributes directly to helping our clients make the best games possible. Tasks We are looking for an experienced Project Manager (f/m/d) to help us expertly manage remote playtesting for game studios around the world. Our chosen candidate will: Take ownership of the successful planning, execution and delivery of remote mobile playtests, including scoping, quoting, technical setup and evaluation of the results Communicate effectively with our clients via email and Zoom to provide project-specific support for their playtesting needs Set up complicated playtests, schedule interviews and facilitate communication between clients and external Games User Researchers, all while meeting tight deadlines and managing multiple projects simultaneously Master and use in-house and external software programs to facilitate playtesting and client communication, including Asana, Jira, Slack, and Heroku Document and regularly update information for playtests and processes related to playtesting Suggest improvements that will make our processes and tools more efficient Requirements You have (near) native spoken and written English skills. German is not required for the role. Any additional languages will be a plus. You have 3+ years of experience managing projects, ideally in gaming, user research, research operations or in SaaS businesses You’re able to respond quickly to the needs of our clients and have communicated closely with customers in previous roles You have strong organizational skills, with excellent and proven time management abilities and can manage multiple projects with short deadlines You can quickly learn new complex software. In-house we use our own (custom-built) tools and are looking for someone who has professional familiarity with other complex software or programming languages (e.g. Google Data Studio, Logic Pro X, Unity, Tableau. SQL specifically is a plus) You’re familiar with video games, whether it was “back in the day” or more recently, and are eager to learn more You're open to working a later shift (11:00 - 20:00 Berlin time) All candidates, who may not have the above-listed years of experience, but can demonstrate the necessary skills, may apply and will be considered. With your application, please send us a cover letter on why you are a great fit for PlaytestCloud.
