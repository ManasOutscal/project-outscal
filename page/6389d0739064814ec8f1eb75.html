Job Description

Over the last two years we have seen huge organic growth at Team17 as well as the acquisition of two studios and three major IP’s, and we aren't stopping there. With ambitious growth plans afoot, bringing onboard dynamic, inventive and impassioned Teamsters is central to our success. To support our growth, we are looking for a an innovative Marketing Analyst to join us in the marketing team to work across a multitude of diverse videogames, to provide data which is easy to digest to inform the wider marketing strategy. The person will report into the Head of Marketing, collaborating with Data Analytics, Game Scouting, Marketing and Sales. Critical to the role is a passion for providing market and player insights to address key strategic and tactical questions around establishing audiences, informing marketing campaigns and audience understanding. This is an opportunity to feed into the very fundamentals of what games we choose to sign and how the market changes affect current projects. What you will do Strategic analysis to identify trends and evaluate opportunities for growth utilising a variety of data sources, proactively driving recommendations to better satisfy player and market needs Collaborate with internal teams to establish and meet ongoing player and business needs Track, report, and analyse the performance of marketing activities, delivering regular reports to key stakeholders within the business Work with Brand to help set key marketing KPI’s and being a key role in the washup assessing whether the goals have been achieved Perform deep dives into specific game features to understand the impact on players Keep teams aware and informed of potential region-specific challenges and trends giving teams the ability to adapt campaigns as and when required Provide in-depth guidance and support for a diverse, complex range of queries/requests and promote good practice in the use of data Manage and service data requests putting in place a process used by teams across the business to be adhered to, so all data needs are met Actively seek out data partners we could potentially work with to gain more insightful outcomes What you will need Strong experience within a data analysis role with the ability to deliver data in a presentable and digestible way Excellent knowledge and experience of working with analytics, steam spy, Newzoo, steam DB Advanced knowledge of data sources for videogames Demonstrable experience of planning and managing market research projects from briefing to the delivery of insights Experience of developing and carrying out market and product analysis independently that supports key areas of portfolio development and strategic priorities Proficient in Microsoft software e.g., PowerBI, Excel Excellent analytical skills and attention to detail Some of the benefits you will receive: Annual Bonus, pension matched up to 8%, Share Save Scheme and £1000 of free shares for all existing and new colleagues! BUPA private medical insurance including cover for pre-existing conditions and BUPA Dental Work Anywhere! From home, via one of our 3 offices or a combination of the two. Game Console loans of up to £500, Game Keys, free use of arcade games, pool tables, Gaming competitions. Oh and look out for a range of treats sent to your home throughout the year celebrating Life at Team 17 and our successes! Team17 is an equal opportunities employer with a commitment to building a diverse workforce, representative of the world we live and game in! Accessibility is at the heart of our games and we reflect that in how we operate as a business. Should you require adjustments or flexible arrangements to interview, or take up a role at Team17, please let us know in the notes section of your application and we will accommodate to the best of our ability.
