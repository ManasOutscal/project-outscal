Job Description

This role will enable you to lead production and operations of the entire Team17 USA portfolio. You will aid our developers in the creation and execution of their respective roadmaps, ensuring milestones are tracked and delivered in a timely manner. However, development needs may shift and it’s important to work with developers through the creative process, while ensuring there is always a clear and timely path forward. This role includes, but is not limited to, the following responsibilities: Oversee the execution of milestones for multiple titles in Team17 USA’s portfolio of mobile games Assist developers with roadmap panning, handling resourcing, managing priorities, and achieving deliverables in a timely manner. Work closely with third party contractors and vendors to assist our developers with their various needs, including QA, localization, engineering, and art production, in a cost-effective manner Partner with our internal development and marketing teams to support our developers as needed Help identify bottlenecks in the project workflow/schedule Help identify issues, provide options for resolution of owned areas Experience: Experience of scoping, scheduling, and project management for Mobile games Ability to prepare both short and long term schedules and planning Proactive & willing to take action; responds quickly to challenges Builds strong relationships both within and outside the organization Maintains high standards despite pressing deadlines Effectively balances priorities & spends time on what’s most important
