Job Description

Remote - Americas WHO WE ARE At Fortis Games we aspire to make great games that bring people together while redefining how game companies work. We believe in building a sense of belonging through our games, their communities, and how we operate and treat each other. Through our game communities, we will create powerful connections and lasting memories. We will foster a culture of diversity, equity and belonging where together our diverse skills, experiences and background impact the games we make. We are an early but mighty organization with a leadership team of game industry veterans. There are many opportunities for you to have a big impact on the products we’ll be making as well as the overall direction of the company. If you’re passionate about tackling difficult problems with direct and thoughtful communication and team first mentality, we may be the right place for you. OVERVIEW The Community Development team is looking for a Head of Community Development Operations to be a key strategic contributor and drive the processes, systems, and playbooks that enable the Community Development team to create and support new lanes between our development teams and external communities. This role will report directly to the VP of Community Development. ABOUT THE ROLE Work directly with the VP of Community Development and Community Leads to organize, define, and nurture the community culture within Fortis and our external communities. Drive the creation and implementation of the processes to support the community teams within Fortis, specifically standardizing community outreach & feedback efforts, establishing qualitative EMV efforts, measuring community health, creating playbooks, and team resource management Help build, lead, and nurture the growth of a team of project managers capable of defining, documenting, and implementing processes throughout the Community organization to support our external community needs Share learnings with the broader organization and contribute to our community strategy's overall development and involvement in the Fortis portfolio of games. WHAT YOU’LL ACHIEVE You will become a key voice for all Community personnel and ensure quality, consistent feedback about Fortis community culture, leadership, and general operations. You will help coordinate the day-to-day operations of the Community Development team and develop processes that reinforce the company vision, mission, and values: Developing an internal communication cadence and format. Build and maintain community playbooks to establish and maintain best practices across all of Fortis’ community initiatives Establish best-in-class feedback mechanisms that help inform the community and development teams through surveys, in-person meetings, early market validation, and more. Work with Community Leads to create an assessment framework for evaluating the health of communities as they progress through various phases of our products You will assist Community Leads and Game Directors in creating community plans, laddering up organizational goals and determining resources for these projects Support senior leadership in evaluating community personnel and leveling them appropriately. You will coordinate with community teams to ensure the seamless integration of community initiatives in our external communities and internal development teams You will become an expert in effectively managing a complicated innovation organization. WHAT YOU’LL NEED TO BE SUCCESSFUL A holistic view of organizational best practices around communication, transparency, and culture – particularly as they apply to community engagement and disciplines. A willingness to experiment and craft new means of connecting with our external communities and fostering strong relationships. Ability to communicate your processes to persuade senior leaders, community leads, and development leads of their value and efficacy. Ability to articulate the nuances of various approaches to organizational development and the tradeoffs associated with different paths Ability to synthesize feedback from community teams and refine strategy and approach to ongoing development. Enthusiasm for directing, mentoring, and otherwise elevating the people around you to do their best work. A natural curiosity and eagerness to learn - we believe this is essential to our ability to stay ahead of the market and value this over years of experience. We expect our leaders to continue being students of their craft. You should be educated on the latest trends and strongly understand how they relate to our current and future projects. WHY JOIN US There are many reasons to join us, but here are a few: We strongly believe we are changing how games studios operate and at the core of what we do is making great games that create a connected community Culture of diversity and inclusion and support to all team members Work with some of the industry's coolest people who have designed and launched the best games in the world Fortis is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
