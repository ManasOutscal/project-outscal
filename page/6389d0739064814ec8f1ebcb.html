Rocksteady is looking for a Lead Rendering Programmer to join our dedicated and collaborative team to work on our upcoming AAA title Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League. The role involves leading, supporting and providing technical guidance to our team of experienced rendering programmers. Working closely with the Rendering Director and other tech leads, you’ll help to ensure the team delivers the systems needed to create the highest quality game experience for our players in line with the artistic vision of our Art Director. You’ll also be applying your technical expertise directly by writing code, making optimisations, and resolving complex and challenging issues. This role can be offered on a remote, hybrid working, or studio based basis. Responsibilities Leading, supporting and providing technical guidance for our team of experienced rendering programmers Working with Production to ensure success in hitting our timelines through effective short- and long-term planning Developing rendering features and systems Profiling, optimising and helping set budgets Coming up with creative solutions to support content creators Leading by example in development best practices Maintaining and improving team effectiveness and efficiency Requirements A high level of technical ability, including with C++ Professional game development experience on high quality projects A broad and comprehensive understanding of rendering techniques Knowledge of a variety of CPU, GPU and asset optimisation approaches Self-directed, organised and able to prioritise effectively Bonus Points Experience with Unreal Engine 4 Familiarity with the latest generation of consoles The Perks Exclusive WarnerMedia events and advance screenings Paid time off every year to volunteer for eligible employees Access to well-being tools, resources, and freebies Access to in-house learning and development resources Part of the WarnerMedia family of powerhouse brands WarnerMedia embraces the opportunity to build a workforce that reflects the diversity of our society and the world around us.
