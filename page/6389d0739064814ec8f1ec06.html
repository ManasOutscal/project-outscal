Job Description

Want to be onboard a rocketship and help build a new and groundbreaking MMORPG called Ember Sword? Senior Network Developer As a Senior Network Developer on the Bright Star Studios engineering Team, you'll be part of the core infrastructure team. You will work on implementing and optimizing network solutions for Ember Sword and its surrounding services. We are expanding with rocket speed, while being on our quest to make the next big MMORPG. Generally, building something from scratch should excite you rather than terrify you. Don't worry, you won't be doing this alone; throughout the last year our team grew by 125% and we have various backgrounds from companies such as Riot Games, Blizzard, Apple, Unity, Lucas Art, and so on. We have plenty of ideas and we are looking for more talented people to help us fulfill them. Responsibilities: Implementing and maintaining RPC system, maintaining message packing, optimizing network stack Analyzing, designing and configuring network infrastructure and application components Work closely with DevOps to closely monitor applications in live environment and improve their stability and performance. Create internal debug tools to inspect network messages and give insight into the network stack Work with game developers to create features and tools based on their requests About Bright Star Studios We are crazy ambitious. That’s one of the reasons why Ember Sword is created on our own custom game engine. The game should be playable via a browser and it is a matter of seconds from you entering our site until you are ingame. If you want to play in another region, you don’t need to start from scratch. You’ll simply have all your gear, levels, and items with you - This means you can always play with your friends, regardless of what server you both started on. No more annoying scenarios where you have to make new characters or pay to move to another server. All your skins are owned by the players, not the gaming companies. All cosmetics are dropped in-game as free NFT’s, allowing the player to truly own it, and enables them to legally trade assets outside the game. Cosmetics have zero in-game advantages and you can not pay to win. It’s free to play and it’s a game by gamers, to gamers. We are able to proudly state that, as our team consists of former professional Counter-Strike and World of Warcraft players. Everyone is invited: Because together we are better. It's our policy to provide equal employment opportunities for all applicants and members of Bright Star Studios. We know that fresh and varied perspectives will make us better at what we do, so however you identify and whatever background you bring with you, we're excited to hear from you. Don't be discouraged if you feel you don't meet every one of the requirements for a role, there's always room for growth at Bright Star Studios. You can work remote or join us in one of our offices located in Denmark, Netherlands, Serbia or Romania. We can even assist with relocation if you please. Listen to our podcast to know more about us, we recommend "Working at Bright Star Studios" and "Meet The Team", to understand more about our culture If you spot a role to make you want to jump out of bed in the morning, we are waiting to hear from you! Requirements The MUSTs: Expertise C++ 5+ years experience with network stacks Solid understanding of TCP/IP and UDP packets Experience with profiling, input validation / sanitization, fuzzing of network packets The GOOD TO HAVEs: Knowledge of the uWebSockets library . Knowledge of WebTransport Genuine love for MMOs and/or RPGs Experience with data driven programming principles (Data driven development) Benefits We offer remote options and flexible working hours. We offer balance between work and personal life and are encouraged with open paid time off. All employees getting onboard 2022 will get a token package, and therefore have an incentive to make the best MMORPG. Besides that, we have the usual: Free coffee and tea. Social gatherings. Board game nights. Free office snacks and soft drinks. Friday bars. Health insurance.
