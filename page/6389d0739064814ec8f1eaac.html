Job Description

Climax Studios was established nearly 30 years ago, we have an extensive history in developing all kinds of digital experiences across all formats, and we have won multiple awards along the way. We have a state of the art studio located in the heart of the beautiful Gunwharf Quays in Portsmouth overlooking the Harbour. You take your creativity seriously and games should be what get you fired up and passionate. At Climax we embrace those who see things in a different way and aren’t afraid to try-out new things and push themselves out of their comfort zones. We are seeking a fully remote Senior Level Designer, with proven skills & experience working on PC & Console titles, to join our award-winning team. This is an exciting opportunity to work on a AAA IP, where you will have the opportunity to demonstrate your abilities throughout a diverse range of features, from level design & flow, to controls & usability. This is a fantastic opportunity to work with a team of passionate and experienced game designers on a AAA project for a 1st party publisher. Requirements A strong grasp of flow, balancing & pacing. Experience balancing gameplay systems. Experience working within UE4, as well as with Blueprints. An understanding of UXD. Experience greyboxing levels. Experienced making gameplay passes (Area Collisions/Event Triggers/Out of bounds volumes etc.) Experience with enemy placement/spawn points/navigation & behaviours. Excellent communication skills to collaborate with a creative & multidisciplinary team. Perforce skills are a benefit. RESPONSIBILITIES Keep to the project's requirements for best practices & methodologies. To champion the game's vision. Constantly assess gameplay, flow & feel to the desired UX. Ability to review & feedback on the team’s work in a clear & constructive manner. Able to work with critique & come up with solutions. Ability to self-motivate as-well-as work well as part of a team and mentor the more junior members of the team. Enjoys a creative challenge with the passion & motivation to make great games. Benefits Regular social events Cycle To Work Scheme Pension Fresh fruit and snacks A friendly, focused, ambitious and relaxed working environment Ongoing training and development, with career progression Discount card for all the shopping centre stores Great seafront location, a short walk from the rail station, bars, restaurants, coffee shops Group Private Medical Insurance provided by BUPA Group Life Insurance Employee Assistance Programme Private Dental Insurance Group Income Protection Company Pension Scheme Cycle To Work Scheme Eye-Care Vouchers Perks Bring your doggo to work! We're a dog friendly studio, bring your pooch in to meet us. Bean to cup coffee machine in the studio. Fresh fruit and healthy snacks provided daily, breakfast and some not so healthy snacks. In-house clubs and socials. From board games, tennis, football to D&D nights, there are lots of opportunities to socialise. Regular Socials including Project Wrap Parties, team and studio events, Christmas and annual Summer parties and soem great charity fundraising events.
