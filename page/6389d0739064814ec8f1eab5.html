Job Description

ABOUT THE POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES Provide support to end users in the home Site and corporate sites on all local IT hardware and software issues. Install, configure and maintain local IT hardware and software according to policies in place. Identify the cause of hardware and software failures and provide corrective action to rectify the problem. Identify solutions which will reduce or eliminate the re-occurrence of hardware and software problems Ensure email and other communication systems are operating properly, efficiently, and in accordance with corporate security and retention policies. Answer queries and provide OS and Office application support to end users. Assist in the development of and to adhere to standardised hardware and software installation and support procedures. Provide support to other business units in the company as directed by IT management Ensure IT Issues are being resolved in a timely manner according to the policies in place. Maintain asset database, maintenance contracts and carry out regular hardware and software audits. Document and maintain accurate installation and procedure guides to the local site. Keeping the Server room and IT area organised and tidy. Ability to work calmly in high pressure situations and manage multiple ongoing projects. Building and Developing Relationships Develop a strong relationship and trust with all local staff Establish and maintain a good team relationship within the global IT team Collaboration with related internal departments To build and enhance relationships with all territories and suppliers KEY SKILLS 2+ years’ experience with the Windows operating system, MS Active Directory and MS Exchange (including DHCP, DNS, etc.), TCP/IP based networks, workstations and infrastructure hardware. Knowledge of Apple Mac, IOS, as well as server and PC workstation hardware, software and security essential. Proven excellent customer service skills Attention to detail and high degree of accuracy in recording and reporting Excellent communication skills Proven track record of Supporting Microsoft and Apple products in a networked environment Strong knowledge of Microsoft applications Ability to interact with end users to successfully resolve queries and problems Ability to translate technical jargon into plain English Understanding of ITIL processes. TRAINING REQUIREMENTS Any related to job responsibilities COMPANY SYSTEMS & PROCESSES Laptop/Desktop Hardware Microsoft Windows 10 Microsoft Office 2016/O365 Microsoft Exchange 2016 Mac OS Active Directory Experience of supporting iPhones LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS Commercial English PERFORMANCE MEASURES Local user satisfaction with IT service Quality of local IT infrastructure Responsiveness to calls Timekeeping Meeting project deadlines Regular reports to IT line management
