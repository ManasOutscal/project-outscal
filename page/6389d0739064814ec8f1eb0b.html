Job Description

Bitwise Alchemy is a consulting studio that solves the tough problems for AAA studios and other game tech companies. Our growing team has decades of experience in AAA video game development building and leading large projects. We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to work with us on exciting initiatives with major industry partners. Unlike traditional studios, we offer a unique way to leverage your game development skills: WE OFFER Industry leading salary At least one month severance PTO Benefits and 401k match (US only) REQUIREMENTS 7+ years of software engineering experience. Strong C++ skills. A desire to explore new projects and solve tough problems. DESIRED SKILLS Unreal Engine 4 Graphics Animation Optimization Networking and Multiplayer Backend service development UI development Math Multiple platforms (Consoles, Mobile, PC) General game dev experience (tools, gameplay, AI, etc.) OTHER Never crunch. Our developers have a maximum number of hours they can work each month (approx. 160h), minus any holidays or PTO. Flexible work schedule that respects your other commitments. For example, it would be fine to take every Friday off and make up the hours on other days. Live and work where you want. We are a remote-only company.
