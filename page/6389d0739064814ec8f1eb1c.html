Job Description

Player First Games is building best-in-class online game experiences for a passionate playerbase. Each member joining our team will have a significant impact on the direction of the game and growth of the company. As the Senior Gameplay Engineer, you will: Responsibilities Own technical planning, implementation, and delivery of features from conception to completion Empower world-class creatives to jointly create unforgettable moments Drive a high-quality bar for all code, content, and systems Mentor fellow team members Provide technical leadership for code architecture, content systems, best practices, and more Skills Strong critical thinking and problem-solving abilities Communication and collaboration skills Experience Understanding of common data structures and algorithms C++ experience Experience owning significant features from start to finish 5 years of relevant professional experience Deep subject matter expertise in at least one area of game engineering Preferred Qualifications Unreal Engine 4 experience Shipped titles in the game industry 10+ years of relevant professional experience Mentorship, leadership, or management experience We offer Competitive compensation and benefits Shared success in the company World-class talent with experience on some of the industry’s biggest games, including God of War(™), League of Legends(™), Overwatch(™), and Call of Duty(™) A culture focused on player trust and player experience Please note: Our teams are working remotely during the current COVID-19 outbreak and until circumstances allow for a safe return. Must be authorized to work in the United States and available to work from our home office when circumstances allow. At Player First Games we design experiences from the player’s perspective first. We hold our players in the highest regard and hope to leave them satisfied and delighted with every interaction. Player First Games is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
