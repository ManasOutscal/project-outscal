Job Description

Remote - Americas WHO WE ARE At Fortis Games we aspire to make great games that bring people together while redefining how game companies work. We believe in building a sense of belonging through our games, their communities, and how we operate and treat each other. Through our game communities, we will create powerful connections and lasting memories. We will foster a culture of diversity, equity and belonging where together our diverse skills, experiences and background impact the games we make. We are an early but mighty organization with a leadership team of game industry veterans. There are many opportunities for you to have a big impact on the products we’ll be making as well as the overall direction of the company. If you’re passionate about tackling difficult problems with direct and thoughtful communication and team first mentality, we may be the right place for you. ABOUT THE ROLE As the Engineering Manager, Games Backend Services you will manage a team of engineers developing the online backend services for our games. You will collaborate with game development teams, product managers, operations teams, devops and security teams. You will assist in scope definition, planning, work estimation and tracking; evaluate and recommend tools, technologies and processes to ensure the highest quality product platform. You will support the development of an evolutionary roadmap aligned with new strategic initiatives; collaborate with game development teams, quality assurance and end users to produce cutting-edge software solutions. You will interpret business requirements to articulate the business needs to be addressed. WHAT YOU’LL ACHIEVE Lead team of engineers to develop shared backend service features for our games including systems such as remote configuration, identity system, guilds and chat systems, etc Responsible and accountable for team efficiency, communication, quality and predictability by setting up, maintaining and optimizing best practices, and continuously leveling up the team and organization Scope the direction, growth, success criteria and accountability for individuals on a team Drive roadmap creation with deep partnership with the Product org and responsible for execution of the engineering team Measure impact of team and set clear expectations and goals Work effectively with cross functional partners and stakeholders to set and achieve optimal outcomes Partner with leadership to influence and drive organizational design, contribution, and prioritization You will spend less time writing code, but there is an expectation that you still engage in small technical deliverables such as bug fixes and small features, without blocking or slowing down the progress of their team. More than writing code, you will draw from your ample experience building highly scalable backend systems to drive the technical direction of our shared backend services, and be a technical leader and a sounding board to members of your technical team Partner with the product team to manage scope and deliverables for the technical side of the product roadmap Work with team to scope and stage work into well defined milestones to avoid monolithic deliverables Identify and solve cross cutting technical issues and team issues to achieve business goals Identify and proactively tackle technical debt through cost/benefit analytics for eliminating debt and suggested timelines for how to prioritize it Provide guidance and model what good looks like for engineering leadership and processes Responsible for your team’s productivity and impact WHAT YOU’LL NEED TO BE SUCCESSFUL: Proven experience scaling yourself effectively through delegation and leadership, excels at getting the team to focus on highest-impact problems Experience making a positive impact on a company’s technical infrastructure or direction through influence and/or leadership A thorough understanding of cloud-native architectures, microservice development and operational best practices in the cloud Strong track record of implementing services in a variety of distributed computing environments Hands on expertise with Kubernetes container platforms in cloud-native environments Hands-on expertise developing and working with RESTful APIs Strict adherence to developing with unit and integration testing in mind Proficiency with JavaScript/TypeScript, with knowledge of C#, Java, and GoLang a plus Fully comfortable with agile management practices, and a proven project manager who has delivered many large projects successfully from planning to multiple delivery milestones to a successful V1.0 An independent manager. Comfortable managing team members with different skill sets from their own. You communicate expectations clearly to all team members, solicit and deliver individual feedback frequently Strong communication skills across a variety of disciplines including with product, engineering, stakeholders, and QA Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, a related technical field, or equivalent practical experience WHY JOIN US There are many reasons to join us, but here are a few: We strongly believe we are changing how games studios operate and at the core of what we do is making great games that create a connected community Culture of diversity and inclusion and support to all team members Work with some of the industry's coolest people who have designed and launched the best games in the world Fortis is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
