Job Description

PeopleFun™ is the award-winning creator of mobile games for players across the globe. We are part of the AppLovin™ studios family, a publicly traded company, with world-class projects, resources, and infrastructure. PeopleFun™ is a Dallas-based game studio founded and maintained by game industry veterans, where game developers thrive on collaboration, creativity, teamwork and fun. Our games are played by over 25 million players each month, and we have a number of exciting mid-core and casual games in development. Our mission is twofold: excellence in creativity and data-driven product innovation, and to be a best-in-class workplace providing unique opportunities to learn and exercise curiosity. Candidates who reside or will reside in the following locations are eligible for this role: California, Nevada, Oregon, Texas, Colorado, Washington State, Ohio, North Carolina, Florida, Pennsylvania, Washington DC, Michigan, New York, Mississippi, Georgia, Maryland, Iowa or Toronto. PeopleFun is seeking a highly skilled Senior Unity Graphics Engineer to join our client engineering team. This role will focus on rendering pipeline and graphics frameworks on top of the Unity Engine. You will collaborate on game features to deliver graphics based solutions for a variety of different gameplay and behavior. You will be responsible to ensure that our games look and run great on the target devices Responsibilities Code in C# and HLSL Develop the graphics pipeline and shaders library in Unity Profile and optimize performance bottlenecks Collaborate with the art team to support the art assets importing and decide on art assets constraints Produce high quality, well-documented code promoting modularity, extensibility and performance Work with artists and developers to ensure proper usage of graphics pipeline within performance budget Develop Unity Editor tools for artists and game designers to achieve desired visuals Requirements 5+ years of expert level experience in one of Obj-C, C, C++ or C# 3+ years of working in Unity Experience in writing, editing and optimizing shaders Familiar with 3D math Self directed, can identify problems and tackle large features with minimal guidance Shipped multiple games on Android and iOS Bonus Points Proficient in C# Experience with core Unity systems including Animators, UI, Batching, Lighting, etc Experience with writing Unity Editor tools Experience working with Addressable Assets and Asset Bundles Knowledge of import formats and texture settings in the engine. (ETC2 PVRTC ASTC).
