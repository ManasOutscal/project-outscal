Job Description

Goodgame Studios is a leading free-to-play, online games development company. We operate across web and mobile platforms and cater to over 500 million registered users worldwide. With employees from more than 40 nations, we are among the largest German employers in the gaming software industry. Since 2018, Goodgame Studios has been part of Stillfront Group AB, which is a group of independent developers, publishers and distributors of digital games - with a vision to become the leading group of game developers and publishers. We are convinced that only the best people will create the best products and drive a company towards success. We believe that teamwork, expertise and bringing different perspectives together are the heart and soul of game development. Conquer the gaming world collectively with the greatest colleagues and the most exciting company around - become a part of our family! As an UI/UX Designer - Freelancer (all genders), you will have the opportunity to be responsible for the UI/UX design of our games. You will work together with the game design, art and development teams to create design systems and produce user interfaces for our games. Your good communication skills and your positive attitude help you to achieve success. Apply now and join us as a freelancer in this full-time project for approximately six months. YOUR MISSION Independently develop and apply methods to achieve great player experience in our games Take responsibility for the UI/UX design of a product in cooperation with the Game Design, Art and Development teams Create and develop wireframes, mock-ups, user flows, prototypes and produce UI for our games Support in creating and applying design systems to achieve consistency across the project Applying best practices in UI/UX for mobile devices Advocate for the users and promote the user-centric mindset across the team YOUR BACKSTORY Several years of professional experience in user experience design, concept development, interaction design, information architecture Completed degree in Media Psychology, Graphic Design, Game Design or similar qualification Extensive experience in Adobe CC suite (Adobe Illustrator and Adobe XD in particular) Experience in UI/UX design for mobile products A self-motivated individual with a passion for online and casual games Excellent analytical skills and a strong customer focus Well organized, solid communication skills and a positive team player attitude Familiar with industry standards and emerging trends in software Very good written and spoken English SOUNDS GREAT? MORE REASONS TO TAKE THIS MISSION Full flexibility: Work remotely or come and work from our modern office in Hamburg with roof terrace, cafeteria, common rooms, gaming and lounging etc. or take a lap in the swimming pool on our beautiful campus. Join a supportive team in a pragmatic, yet challenging environment, flat hierarchies and state of the art technologies. Work with multidisciplinary and experienced colleagues from the entire product lifecycle in a culture of continuous development and learning opportunities. Don't be hungry ever again: there are fresh fruits, snacks and drinks available at the office. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION Please provide us with a current UI/UX portfolio as a file or link with your application. The safety of our employees and everyone else is of utmost importance to us which is why you will experience a solely virtual recruiting process. We are happy to receive your application in English! #Stayhome #Stayhealthy Your contact person for this job offer is Simon Guenther. Goodgame Studios is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage and support diversity and welcome all qualified applications.
