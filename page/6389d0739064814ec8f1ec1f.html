Job Description

Babel, a Keywords Studio is the leading international provider of outsourcing services to games and interactive entertainment industries. Founded in 1999, the company is part of the renowned Keywords Group, an international technical service provider to the global Video Game Industry. The company provides a complete outsourced testing and localization service for all Console, PC, Handheld and Mobile content, to many of the biggest names in games and interactive entertainment, working on thousands of titles including many of the best-selling titles of the past few years. Babel India currently provides Functionality Games testing on PC, Console and mobile platforms services to leading Games Industry companies in Europe and North America. It also provides project management services for translation services (called Localisation project management). For more info please refer to our website. Key Responsibilities and Activities: Automation Strategy Planning- Identification of Automation candidates Monitor the Automation runs for failures if any and report Defects for the failures in the automation run. Collaborate with multiple teams to ensure Automation is implemented and maintained with Quality. Enhance test coverage including Robustness and Failure Path coverage. Promote re-use between existing Code base and new applications. Collaboration with stakeholders Work with QA and Design team members for enhancing and providing assistance on various requests associated with enhancement to our Automation Framework Work with QA members to provide help/assistance on a per-need basis Work with the Design team to expedite Defect Fixes where required Requirements Software testing Working experience in development of test strategy, test planning, manual & automation test development. Worked in an Agile development environment with experience testing either In-scrum or customer vertical use cases, executing cross functional workflows using API micro services. Technical Experience identifying and working with Test Automation tools ex. Selenium, Cucumber, Silk test, Robot Framework etc. Experience with automation framework using Xcode with XCUITest and SwiftUI technologies. Experience with automation framework using Flutter Integration with Visual Studio and Dart technologies. Experience with Repository manager and Build management tools. Development experience of Python, JavaScript, Postman and Selenium. Knowledge of databases like Oracle, MySQL. Working knowledge of Enterprise based network management software solutions in a Unix/Linux/VM/Cloud based environment. Understanding of Test and Defect Management tools. Soft skills Excellent communication and inter-personal skills. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Experienced in coordination of activities between global teams. Team player and leader to enable wholesome participation and growth within the team. Independent self-starter and committed to delivering on aggressive deadlines. Comfortable in a team environment with ability to lead or to work with little supervision. Ability to prioritize within the scope of responsibility, multi-task and manage time effectively. Self-motivated and pro-active with attention to detail. Process Experience with Agile software development methodologies. Knowledge of general software Product Development process and methodologies. Desirable Characteristics Strong debugging skills. Ability to understand customer's perspective. Willingness and ability to Learn. Experience required: Industry experience of 2 years to 4 years.
