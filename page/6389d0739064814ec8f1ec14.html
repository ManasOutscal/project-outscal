Job Description

PerBlue is looking for an experienced and passionate Director of Engineering to lead our experienced game developer teams. We’re a growing independent studio whose free-to-play midcore mobile games have been played by millions of people around the world, including the award-winning Disney Heroes: Battle Mode. Our team brings together experience from major video game studios and technology companies. Headquartered in Madison, Wisconsin, we also work remotely around the United States and Canada for an unmatched quality of life and work. You’ll play an important part in creating amazing experiences for our fans across the world. This new position for our studio will manage our front-end, gameplay/engine, and back-end development teams. You’ll help us develop better processes, provide great ideas for our teams’ career development, and help develop an evolved vision for our development and delivery infrastructure. We’re seeking solid experience in creating applications and systems that are used by millions of people. You’ll drive consensus among our engineering team leaders to solve problems and develop forward-looking systems and processes. You’ll lead meaningful technical discussions about all aspects of application development as a true technology leader, someone with innovative ideas and the ability to lead and enhance our high-performing team of technologists. Requirements 10+ years of hands-on bare-metal application coding experience to include 4+ years of technology team leadership Expertise with architecting and optimizing distributed systems Practical knowledge of technology team development, including career pathing, professional development, and retention Your Attributes A passion for all things related to architecture and programming with amazing technical skills Strong remote management skills; this position will manage a remote-first team Strong attention to detail and a constant desire to learn new things Be results-oriented and able to thrive in a fast-paced environment Outstanding written and verbal communications skills in a highly collaborative, remote work environment A focus on product and an eye for quality Proactive and able to take ownership of tasks and projects Active participation in PerBlue’s efforts to be a welcoming, inclusive and diverse workplace Other Preferred Skills and Experience Experience managing team leads/managers Cloud experience Prior experience at a game studio (mobile, console, or PC) Compensation and Benefits We are a Remote First company headquartered in downtown Madison, Wisconsin with team members based around the US. We offer a competitive base salary; incentive bonus structure; 6+ weeks of paid time off; comprehensive health, dental, vision, disability, and life insurance; a well-matched 401k; flexible hours; and a variety of other perks including daily lunch delivery. You'll be part of a passionate, welcoming, and collaborative team that has received several “Best Place to Work” awards by a variety of publications. We are headquartered in the vibrant neighborhood around beautiful downtown Madison. No agencies, please. This is a pretty good description of this position’s roles and responsibilities but is not a comprehensive job description, so duties and supervisors may change. We regret that we cannot consider visa sponsorship or candidates based outside of the United States or Canada for this position at this time.
