Job Description

Come join the team at Zynga making an impact across all of the company’s games - Central Technology! On the TechEnablement team, we focus on shared client & server tech that drives the social gameplay, compliance and messaging solutions for games. We’re looking for outstanding leaders with a passion for technology and the ability to work with a diverse team. The Engineering Manager position will play a key role in leading teams supporting the development of services/tools for our games. Main Responsibilities: Manage and lead a team of software engineers across different time zones working on various shared products/services for Zynga’s games Drive technical excellence by validating technology choices, architectural design, and code quality, diving deeper when needed Write technical documentation and architecture designs for review with engineering leadership Review code, design specifications and contribute to coding as needed Enable the team to succeed by identifying gaps, improving engineering process, and providing growth opportunities Be the voice of the team in interacting with various partners across fields both inside and outside of the organization Act as a conduit from executive leadership down to the team, distilling strategic direction into tactical action Collaborate with other technical leaders at Zynga to foster best-in-class development Coordinate closely with other fields (especially Production and Product Management) to ensure alignment against larger organizational goals and shape the roadmap Mentor and coach the engineers in the team helping in their career growth. Desired Skills and Experience: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, or equivalent. 2+ years of engineering management experience and/or experience as a principal/senior engineer mentoring junior developers 6+ years of prior hands-on software development experience Solid understanding of standard methodologies for engineering process, code quality, build/release pipelines, security, and live operations Knowledge of the mobile landscape and emerging technologies Experience with multiple programming languages and platforms (Java, C#, C++, and/or Go) Familiarity with agile development methods and tools Experience with iOS, Android, Unity, or Unreal a plus Experience with cloud technologies (AWS, Kubernetes, serverless, etc.) a plus Excellent verbal and written communication skills Excellent teamwork and collaboration skills What we offer you: Zynga Stock RSUs and Bonus Plan Full medical, dental, vision benefits as well as life insurance Catered lunch daily for onsite office Generous Paid Maternity/Paternity leave Open vacation policy for many employees Flexible working hours on many teams Casual dress every single day Work with cool people!
