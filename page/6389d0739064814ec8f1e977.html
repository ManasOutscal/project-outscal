Job Description

Climax Studios was founded over 30 years ago and we have a state of the art studio located in the heart of the beautiful Gunwharf Quays in Portsmouth overlooking the Harbour. We have an extensive history in developing all kinds of AAA games across all formats, through to co-development and technological problem solving and we’ve won many awards along the way. Our recent projects include Crackdown 3 and the BAFTA award winning Returnal. Climax Studios is looking for a UK based, fully remote Gameplay Animator to join the team. You will create consistently high quality animations for in-game use, have an eye for detail and a feel for what works in game. Requirements The ideal candidate should be able to demonstrate their skills and experience in the following: Experience creating realistic hand-keyed animations Show a sound understanding of animation principles Technically competent and have a thorough understanding of animation processes Respond well to feedback and act upon it Experience in mocap cleanup to a high standard Extensive experience in Motionbuilder, UE and Maya Experience implementing motion capture data Good communicator and at ease working within a team Capable of working to tight deadlines when required Degree, related qualification or equivalent experience in Animation Demonstrate quality animation skills with good timing Strong folio including examples of realistic animation Beneficial Successfully worked with iterative development and rapid gameplay prototyping Current console development experience Responsibilities Implement realistic and fluid animations from Mocap data into UE Create realistic hand-keyed animations and implement into UE Critique and iterate in a cross-discipline group Follow and recreate established art styles Reliably demonstrate an aptitude for the platform technology, asset discipline and pipeline processes Benefits Regular social events Cycle To Work Scheme Pension Fresh fruit and snacks A friendly, focused, ambitious and relaxed working environment Ongoing training and development, with career progression Discount card for all the shopping centre stores Great seafront location, a short walk from the rail station, bars, restaurants, coffee shops Group Private Medical Insurance provided by BUPA Group Life Insurance Employee Assistance Programme Private Dental Insurance Group Income Protection Company Pension Scheme Cycle To Work Scheme Eye-Care Vouchers Perks Bring your doggo to work! We're a dog friendly studio, bring your pooch in to meet us. Bean to cup coffee machine in the studio. Fresh fruit and healthy snacks provided daily, breakfast and some not so healthy snacks. In-house clubs and socials. From board games, tennis, football to D&D nights, there are lots of opportunities to socialise. Regular Socials including Project Wrap Parties, team and studio events, Christmas and annual Summer parties and soem great charity fundraising events.
