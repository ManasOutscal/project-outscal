Job Description

We are looking for a motivated and talented Senior Character Concept Artist with focus on External Development. The Character Concept Artist will be responsible for overseeing the deliveries created by external partners and ensuring the visual direction is carried through into the game to the highest quality. The successful candidate will be expected to provide feedback and manage art deliverables as well as drive the external development content creation of a wide variety organic and non-organic character elements. Candidates must be able to hit the internal standards of art, have good knowledge of Game Development and exceptional oral and written communication skills. *Sample pictures or a link to a webpage, which shows sample work, are required.* Relocation & Remote Work Although we believe living close to our studio helps foster team spirit and stronger bonds between team members, we understand that people have different needs and expectations for their place of residence. You have a choice of three location options, allowing you to work with Crytek from anywhere you wish: Come to our modern headquarters in Frankfurt and receive an attractive relocation package and have access to all of our benefits. If you are already living in a European Union member state, we are able to offer you a permanent work contract and allow you to work remotely as an employee from there. If you are interested in full-time remote work in any other country outside of the European Union, we can offer you a freelance contract arrangement. Responsibilities Supervise and provide clear technical and aesthetic feedback to external teams; Work with Art Director and other artists to ensure high quality and vision of external deliverables; Understand and follow style guides to guarantee the consistency of the game; Iterate on ideas quickly through sketches and thumbnails; Track external development on daily basis and incoming assets; Coordinate with other studio groups and production to achieve project objectives; Creating in-game Characters when needed, following art direction; Work together with stakeholder to define and determine outsourcing needs. Requirements 5+ years experience in game development; Expert understanding of current game art processes, limitations and dependencies; Excellent knowledge of 3D Software Packages and Photoshop; Strong focus on Character designs and deep understanding of human / creature anatomy; High Quality portfolio of gamedev-relevant art; Strong knowledge of understanding of anatomy; The ability to manage and supervise mid-level and junior artists and willingness to carry out mentorship tasks; Ability to deliver and receive constructive feedback; Good English communication and writing skills; Positive, problem-solving and team-oriented attitude; Be proactive, self-motivated and be able to work without direct supervision. Heavy focus on Character Art Keen eye on detail and realism Strong artistic skills (Shapes / colors / proportions) Ability to create new and refine existing designs Pluses Previous experience with external production and/or directing others through written communication; Experience with CRYENGINE; Traditional Art background. What you can expect from us A refreshing yet highly professional atmosphere in a diverse team Flexible work time Free public transportation ticket, which lets you use public transport free of charge 24/7 Supported German lessons for our employees Company language is English. Any additional language is a plus, but not a requirement Extensive assistance with getting visa, work permits and communication with local authorities Relocation support A company apartment for your first few months and help in finding a private apartment ...and much more!
