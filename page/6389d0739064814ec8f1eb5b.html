Job Description

Words With Friends (WWF) is a group of passionate gamers whose mission is to connect the world through games. On our mobile engineering team, you will have an opportunity to take ownership over your work that reaches millions of daily users. We take great pride in our craft, delivering a high-quality service to millions of players every single day. Teamwork is a critical part of how we get there. We believe that with an all-in approach we can deliver a better product, make better decisions, and nurture a better work environment. Everyone here is committed to making our games the best experience for our players. Join us as we pursue challenges developing and operating products at scale, influencing millions of players daily and support our game to achieve the highest levels of success! MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES Work on the Words With Friends mobile client team in Unity Architect, write, and ship elegant and maintainable code Create documentation and tech plans for new architecture/system designs Collaborate with your team to build game features, robust systems and UI that looks great and runs smoothly Follow engineering best practices to ensure code quality, performance and reliability. Conduct regular code reviews to ensure high quality Scope out tasks and systems, looking for future improvements Mentor others on the team to help them succeed DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE BS in Computer Science or Engineering field with 5+ years of development experience Experience with Unity Significant programming experience in C#. Experience in Git or a similar version control system Ability to translate business requirements into technical solutions Excellent written and oral communication skills Good mentoring skills Experience with C++ is a bonus. Experience with backend technologies is a bonus.
