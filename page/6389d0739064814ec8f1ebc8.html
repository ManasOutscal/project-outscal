Job Description

Your future is our future; we are a game development studio that is working at the edge of computer science: graph databases, artificial intelligence, microservice fabrics, cutting-edge graphic engine pipeline support, and an internal vision that will revolutionize our industry. We are looking for rockstar talent to help us deliver our next phase of technology that's very different from what you can currently find in the marketplace. Sure, we have a few ideas and a roadmap, but that is never enough! We need your thoughts and opinions … and we need you … to help us find that magical intersection between content and technology, where we can inspire the industry and the community to achieve something different. We are visualizing a future where you make an impact! Some of us have worked together for over 10 years through multiple companies. As a result, we strive to create long-term relationships that help sustain our success. We continue to add new individuals who aren’t just searching for another stepping stone but a place to put down roots. We love what we do and are looking for passionate people who want to grow, learn, inspire, mentor, and make a difference. We are Pixelberry Studios, and we are a top mobile games developer, creating fun and engaging games with heart since 2012. Yes, we’re a division of Nexon, one of the largest gaming companies in the world, and our studio is proud to have never launched a game that did not land in the App Store’s Top 25 Grossing chart. We invite you to join us in the adventure that will become our shared future. SUMMARY OF POSITION Pixelberry Studios is looking for Senior Software Engineer. Our headquarters are in Mountain View, CA but we can accommodate a remote work environment for a candidate from the following states: California, Colorado, Florida, Maryland, Nevada, Oregon, South Dakota, Texas, and Washington. Currently, all Pixelberry staff work remotely with the option to work in-office, if desired. RESPONSIBILITIES Collaborate with Production, QA team, and teammates to develop new technology Design, code, unit test, and maintain scalable server codebases Research new technologies and help architect the future technology roadmap Develop fun, innovative features used by millions Maintain a mentoring relationship with members of your team Follow and encourage standards around development, code review, testing, and deployment REQUIREMENTS Computer Science degree, or equivalent work experience 3+ years Cocos2Dx and have an interest in Unity 5+ years of modern programming language (Java, C#) experience in an enterprise or server environment 3+ years of database experience (SQL, Dynamo, Neo4J) Experience with the AWS technologies such as Dynamo, Neptune, EventBridge, etc. Solid foundational programming skills (algorithms, data structures, OOP, etc) Ability to solve cutting-edge technical challenges at scale, with performant solutions A passion for games, and an understanding of how they work Good communication and collaboration skills U.S. work authorization BENEFITS & PERKS Competitive salary High level of autonomy and freedom Rich & flexible health, disability, and employee assistance offerings to meet the needs of a diverse workforce 401(K) with matching program Paid Year-End Break For local employees that elect to work in-studio, a relaxed office environment with free food and snacks Virtual escape rooms, video game nights, trivia parties, and other team-building activities throughout the year HOW THIS JOB IS DIFFERENT Learn about the business side of mobile games. That warm fuzzy feeling that you’re not just making a difference at a company, but in the lives of people all over the world.
