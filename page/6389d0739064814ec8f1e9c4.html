Job Description

Deep Silver Volition has been creating wildly original and successful video games for over 25 years, including the fan-favorite Saints Row series. Our studio has prospered by making fun, innovative games no one else can make, while focusing on work-life balance and fostering a culture of collaboration, continuous growth, and learning. Volition is looking for an energetic and passionate Outsourcing Manager to help us with environment art for the next Saints Row title! You will work with a tightly knit group of environment artists and manage content from multiple vendors. This role is critical to the pipelines of the environment team, and therefore requires an organized person who can communicate with people in multiple time zones effectively. This is a remote-friendly role. We’re a hybrid work environment, headquartered in Champaign, IL but with employees working permanently remotely across the U.S. (We’re currently unable to hire in CA & NY, unfortunately.) What you’ll do as an Outsourcing Manager at Volition: Leverage organizational and communication skills to ensure productive partnerships with vendors and pipelines. Coordinate with Art Directors and Leads in developing and documenting style targets, specifications, and deliverables. Drive pipeline improvements by staying on top of industry trends, exploring new tools, and offering recommendations. Assemble job sheets for work to be sent to vendor artists. Organize and provide impactful feedback on assets throughout their creation. Facilitate review and critique meetings with internal and external environment teams. What we’re looking for: Previous experience with outsourcing and asset management, preferably for AAA games. Clear and concise communication, management, and people skills. Demonstrated process orientation, with an understanding of project management methodologies. Ability to create a plan, track tasks, and identify & solve risks and bottlenecks across multiple concurrent projects. Strong interpersonal, verbal & written communication, conflict resolution, diplomacy and facilitation skills. Experience with JIRA and Perforce. Some travel may be necessary to meet and build relationships with external vendors. Shipped titles as an Artist, Art Lead, or Art Director. Pluses: Knowledge of Unreal, Unity or other game engines/editors. Experience in production/project management. Some of our benefits: Subsidized medical/dental/vision insurance Company-paid life, short-term, & long-term disability insurance Generous paid time off (including parental leave and an annual company shutdown between Christmas & New Year's) 401k with company contribution Annual learning & development budget + access to GDC Vault + LinkedIn Learning Paid subscriptions to Headspace, Doordash, Sworkit, and other wellness apps At Deep Silver Volition we believe in fostering an open, collaborative, and diverse environment, and we are proud to be an equal opportunity employer. We will consider all qualified applicants without attention to race, color, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, genetic information, disability, age, veteran status, or military status.
