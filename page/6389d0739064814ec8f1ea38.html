Multiple Locations: Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada Los Angeles, California, United States Orlando, Florida, United States Austin, Texas, United States Seattle, Washington, United States Pasadena, California, United States Redwood City, California, United States. Responsibilities Major design decisions on AAA PC/Console project Own, drive and communicate the vision for major design initiatives, new features, major design overhauls and hold brainstorming/playtest sessions Work closely and unify all other disciplines: engineers, artists and producers to design, tune and implement various game design directions Write proposals for major systems, new game modes and other large design initiatives Lead complex, challenging, impactful game aspects with minimal oversight Analyze internal and external metrics feedback and adjust design or balance the game if necessary Being subject matter expert in Game Design field: drive knowledge sharing on Department and Studio level Contribute to bizdev activities as subject matter expert Collaborate with other designers to ensure product vision is maintained throughout development Disseminate knowledge with other designers Requirements 5-7 years’ experience in Game Design working for AAA titles;
