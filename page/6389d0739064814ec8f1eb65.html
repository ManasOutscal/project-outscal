Job Description

About Pocket Worlds Our mission is to make the metaverse a reality and our vision is a decentralized virtual world built and owned by its citizens. We have been working towards this mission for a decade. We are the creators of Highrise, the first virtual world on mobile, which has seen extraordinary growth over the last few years and now boasts over 20 million users across iOS and Android. Every day, the citizens of Highrise come together to hangout, explore, create, collect, and trade in our metaverse, and we are on the precipice of making history once again with our expansion into Highrise World. About This Role As a Senior Unity Developer, you will be responsible for designing and building the architecture of the next-gen game engine for Highrise. You will be taking what Highrise is today, with its 20 million users, to the next level by building the game engine that will bring our entire world to life. Along the way, you and your team will be building: Highrise Studio. The UGC toolkit for users to craft environments, experiences, and content to populate the metaverse. Highrise Social. The connecting social fabric of our world, this allows our users to meet and connect with each other in brand new ways not possible with any other virtual world. Highrise Marketplace. Completing the loop between creator and consumer, the Highrise Marketplace is bringing a true e-commerce experience to the virtual world. This role is remote, as is the entire company, but applicants from North & South America, Canada are preferred. As Senior Software Developer, you will... Solve a variety of unique technical challenges Have the independence, opportunity, and the end-to-end responsibility to design, build, test and deploy the next-gen game engine and world builder for Highrise. Be a technical bar-raiser for high code quality, architectural designs, and long-term approaches Mentor and develop fellow engineers on the team Design systems and services that are scalable and resilient Collaborate with passionate, Engineers, Product Managers, and other Highrise team members, cross-functionally To be successful in this role, we'd expect you to… Exceptional C# design and programming skills with at least 5 years of software programming experience You must also have at least 3 years of experience in programming with the Unity engine Experience shipping software at a professional level Have experience architecting, designing, and implementing of a wide array of software products Have experience supporting mission-critical applications at scale Be able to work well in a remote development environment Be highly communicative and go out of your way to contribute to the team and help others Bonus points for experience with mobile app development with iOS or Android Bonus point for any side projects or games you’ve built outside of work You'll Love: Meaningful equity in an extremely fast-growing startup Company-sponsored medical and dental insurance Unlimited Vacation policy. Time off is important - we encourage you to take the time you need Education stipend. We deeply believe in learning and self-improvement. We've set aside a budget for every employee to learn additional skills and grow A Highrise Admin badge for your avatar Closing Our goal is to define the future of the metaverse with the product we build, and to grow from tens of millions to billions of users in the process. Pocket Worlds is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees. Join us on this journey and together we will build something incredible.
