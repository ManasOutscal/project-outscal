Every great story has a new beginning. We're excited to announce that Discovery and WarnerMedia have combined to become Warner Bros. Discovery. WBD is a premier global media and entertainment company offering audiences the world’s most differentiated and complete portfolio of content, brands and franchises across television, film, sports, news, streaming and gaming. We’re home to the world’s best storytellers, creating world-class products for consumers. From brilliant creatives to technology trailblazers and beyond, join us as we step into the next chapter. For the past 25 years Monolith Productions has been obsessed with delivering a unique gaming experience for our fans. Starting with Blood, our very first title – all the way to our most recent offering, Middle-earth: Shadow of War, we are constantly pushing the envelope to combine innovative gameplay, inventive narratives, cutting edge tech & AI, and stunning visuals. We are challenging ourselves even further to ensure that our next project is going to be our best yet. That’s where you come in – for us to achieve that, we need the best and brightest talent the industry has to offer. Help us create our next AAA title. The Job As an Advanced AI Engineer, your domain will span much of the codebase as you design and implement new AI systems as needed, extend existing AI systems, and assist with or even implement the interfaces needed to integrate the AI into other gameplay systems. A strong candidate will have a passion for creating engaging non-player characters (NPCs), whether they be opponents, allies, or neutral entities, all utilizing bespoke systems that convey unique awareness of the player’s actions. At the advanced level candidates are expected to be knowledgeable in the full stack of systems needed to provide believable NPC's for a AAA title. The Daily Design, author, and maintain robust and highly optimized systems that meet all design, technical and schedule requirements Work closely with QA and technical leadership to ensure the stability and accuracy of systems by identifying, communicating and resolving defects Author, review and provide feedback on technical and end-user documentation of systems Collaborate with Technical Leadership and other Engineers to help establish overall technical design and architecture Collaborate with Lead Engineer, Technical Art and Game Engineers to identify systems in need of optimization or replacement; make proposals of exciting new systems or technologies Work closely with production staff in building and maintaining realistic schedules and identifying dependencies; coordinate with QA and Leads to ensure successful roll-out of new features to the Team and establish testing and maintenance of systems Review the work of other Engineers and mentor them to improve their knowledge and skills The Essentials 8+ years professional C/C++ programming Designed and implemented major game systems for a published game Experience developing for major consoles (PS5, Xbox Series X, or prior generation) Worked on multi-year, large-scale production game title from concept to ship Ability to collaborate with engineers and content creators to develop robust and fun game-play The Nice to Haves A passion for creating engaging non-player characters Experience developing first or third person action games Expert level knowledge of 3D math Ability to easily communicate technical topics with both engineers and non-technical people alike Experience with Havok AI or other AI pathfinding solutions Warner Bros.
