Role Overview - We are looking for a talented, self-motivated Business Intelligence (BI) Engineer based out of our EA Hyderabad office. This is a key position that will work with game team analysts and Product Managers to plan, design, and build data platforms and enrichment pipelines that will enable and empower data democratization for the game studio. The role is critical to transforming how we use data at Pogo to drive success to our live services capabilities. What a BI Engineer will do on our team: Partner with Pogo analysts to build platforms and data pipelines to those power insights to Product Managers and other game team stakeholders Work with cross-functional teams to understand and translate analytical requirements into data solution designs, then carry those designs through implementation into production. Work with analysts and developers to develop robust and scalable data designs/pipelines that can be easily maintained and ingested by our BI tool (Tableau) to enable data democratization. Drive and enforce strategies to improve our data modeling and information design; guide analysts on creating scalable end-to-end data solutions; and act as a good data citizen to promote good governance. Support analytical processes, including data table/schemas creation, ETL monitoring, query testing, dashboard creation, and reports generation. Develop a detailed knowledge of the underlying data assets and products and provide subject matter expertise on content and current and potential future use of data. Who fits the bill: 2+ years relevant industry experience in a BI-related role. High proficiency in writing SQL queries and knowledge of cloud-based databases like Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery, or other big data solutions. Experience in data modeling, ETL processes, big data, and data warehousing. Experience with business intelligence tools such as Tableau or similar BI tools. Strong technical understanding of data modeling, design, and architecture principles and techniques across master data, transaction data, and derived/analytic data. Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally and build relationships with technical and business partners.
