Job Description

At Regression Games, we are building the next evolution in gaming, where players program artificial intelligence to compete and play at the highest level. Our mission is to define and build the AI-centric gaming and esports scene through platform, games, and community. Our vision is to be the face of AI gaming, with a global presence, a portfolio of first and third party games, and a community to back it up. We are a VC-funded, remote first startup that is building our team of developers. Our goal is to build a company where developers and software engineers love to work - anti-crunch, inclusive, diverse, and excited by our work and vision. At Regression Games, we strive to provide a place where you can grow your skills and experience as a software engineer. We are looking for a Software Engineer who can contribute to the development of our backend, frontend, and/or infrastructure that supports our AI platform and games. You will work on the systems and code that enables players to write and execute their AIs, socialize with friends as they play, and collaborate on bots. We are looking for someone who is scrappy, able to delve into areas outside their comfort zone, and hungry to build something they are proud of. Base Qualifications Enjoys creating prototypes and experimenting quickly Self-driven and willing to define their own tasks, eager to contribute to many different areas of development Experience working on many parts of a tech stack Preferred Qualifications A love for gaming, esports, and AI You thrive in a fast paced environment where learning new information often changes everything BS (or higher, e.g., MS, or PhD) in Computer Science or related technical field involving coding (e.g., physics or mathematics), or equivalent technical experience Responsibilities Contributing to a team culture that prides itself on excellence, innovation, trust, feedback, and respect Work closely with the rest of the team to design and implement our backend, frontend, and/or infrastructure Develop our game integrations with feedback from players, and engage with our community Other Skills Knowledgeable on recent AI gaming advancements Knowledgeable with low code and no code systems Experience working on a video game title as a developer Benefits Unlimited PTO (with a minimum required time of 15 days) Great medical, dental, vision, and life insurance Mental health resources and reimbursement Remote-first (will have office space as we grow) Recognition of work in-game $1000 game and professional development reimbursements 401k Research shows that in order to apply for a job, women feel they need to meet 100% of the criteria while men usually apply after meeting about 60%.
