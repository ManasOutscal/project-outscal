Job Description

The Zynga Blockchain Studio (ZBC) is looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join us on our Web3 journey and help build new games utilizing blockchain technology! WHAT YOU’LL DO You will work closely with team members across disciplines such as Art and Game Design to develop and launch a brand new game within the Web3 space. You create high quality and performant code, providing our players with an enjoyable experience. KEY RESPONSIBILITIES Develop core gameplay systems and interfaces Build tools to enable quality of life improvements for the team Communicate your progress and offer feedback to your teammates REQUIRED SKILLS Previously shipped at least one product in a team environment Experience with version source control (e.g. Git, Perforce, Subversion, etc.) Degree or equivalent experience in Computer Science, Game Development or related field Fluent in written and spoken English DESIRED SKILLS Experience with programming in C#, C++ Knowledge of HTML5 / Javascript Passion for blockchain development (e.g., smart contracts) or crypto games Portfolio of game jams or games development experience ABOUT THE STUDIO ZBC is leading Zynga’s development of Web3 games, blending blockchain technology and decades of combined game development experience across AAA and mobile platforms. Our mission is to empower players to invest, own and earn through fun, high-quality and enduring blockchain games they can trust.
