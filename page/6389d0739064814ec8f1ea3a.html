Job Description

About the position We are looking for a talented and experienced Game Designer to join our team to create new game content and design major features. You will work in close communication with the art, engineering, product, content, and marketing teams, from the beginning of the planning phase till far beyond launch, making sure the project stays true to its vision and delivering high-quality play experience. As a Game Designer you will prototype, test, and document gameplay ideas to clarify and promote collaborative innovation. Translating game concepts into viable specifications and gathering feedback from other teams to improve the design process and continuously enrich our creative environment. Your main responsibilities will include: Create and document new game features Prototype your own game ideas to better convey your concepts Balance game economy and game mechanics using analytics data, simulations and playtests Give feedback to art and development teams Benchmarking Skill requirements Previous Game Design experience Strong knowledge of game design fundamentals and contemporary game trends. Great communication skills and teamwork mentality Familiarity with some scripting language Familiarity with digital metrics and analytics User testing Wireframing and UX fundamentals Bonus Points Experience working with mobile games development, preferably in free-to-play model Familiarity with Agile development and Scrum Did you like the job description? Feel free to apply even if you don't have all the requirements, we are looking for passionate people willing to learn and grow (: What we offer Competitive remuneration Flexible benefits Health and Dental Insurance- Profit Sharing Career development Support Casual environment as well as the dress code A space for resting moments, full of snacks, fruits, and drinks. About Fanatee Fanatee is a mobile gaming startup founded in 2013. Since then, our goal has been to enable people to play our games anytime, anywhere. We believe that quality is the key to success. Our creative processes are well planned and carefully detailed, and our talent lies in turning great ideas into great businesses: our games hit over 130M downloads worldwide. We believe that a diverse work environment contributes to an increasingly responsible society that values differences. Our dream allows us to do what we love the most: work with talented, passionate people and give them the opportunity to achieve goals as big as they can dream of. If you want to share this ride with us and join a lightweight, fun and focused environment that breathes innovation and game culture, apply now!
