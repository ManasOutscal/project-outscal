Job Description

We’re looking for a Pixel Artist contractor to work on our in-development magic school RPG Witchbrook! We are an independent and self-funded indie studio and publisher, with three in-house titles: Starbound, Wargroove and Witchbrook. We have also published: Eastward, Stardew Valley, Risk of Rain, and many more. We are passionate about what we do, and are looking for someone who shares this passion. Our team is very friendly and we take pride in having a no-crunch policy, 4-day work week, and full creative independence. Successful applicants will be joining us to help develop Witchbrook on a contract basis. We are open to applicants from all over the world so long as you can match our in-house hours. To apply please fill in the form below with your CV, portfolio, availability, and day rate. Requirements: Excellent pixel art skills and general 2D art skills Confident character and environment animation (pixel art) Self-motivated and proactive attitude towards work Ability to adapt to different art styles Able to explore and experiment – sketches, mockups etc. based on rough briefs Great communication skills – sharing and receiving feedback and keeping the team up to date with their work Able to match our in-house hours (between 9-to-5 and 10-to-6) Please feel free to let us know if we need to accommodate disability-related requests
