Job Description

Created by Monsters. Built for gamers.™ TLM Partners, Inc. is a world-class digital entertainment company led by industry veterans. We are an engineering powerhouse, and our Subject Matter Experts work on some of the most ambitious projects in the industry and work alongside partners as 'off-site' professional services for publishers, indie video game developers, SAAS, Cloud, and Visualization companies. We are setting the bar for best-in-class cross play gaming experiences on every platform using our custom technologies and tools. We offer a 100% remote working environment as a “Cloud Studio”, with awesome benefits to support a harmonious work life balance from anywhere in the world. Come join our vision to build a world class studio. Position Summary: Technical Director (Engine/Systems) Full time, 100% remote. Develop engine systems as well as improve workflows for our development teams on platforms including PC and consoles. Be a part of a groundbreaking global game developer and publisher, with a permanently remote team. Competitive salary, excellent benefits, and stock options available for full-time employees. Responsibilities: Leads teams of engineers working on projects Expert in engine architectures, systems and how they relate to game design Understand the differences between supported game platforms and smoothly transition between them Lead on evaluating performance, and optimization on supported game platforms for both customer projects as well as TLM’s internal tech. Work closely with the other team leads as well as the customer on technical goals, features, and development. As a leader, ensure that code quality, reusability and documentation are always a high priority. Work with TLM central tech on the roadmap and architecture of our key internal systems. Own the customer facing relationship for your engineering team Work with the CTO and TDs on improving our training, processes and standards in order to grow our internal team Required Qualifications: Master’s Degree in Computer Science or equivalent combination of education and experience. 8+ years developing and optimizing engine systems. 3+ shipped AAA console or PC titles, with at least 2 as a lead/senior/TD Strong C/C++ skills are a must. Adept at using common development, profiling and optimization software. Background with parallel programming and multi-threaded engine systems. Understanding of computer architecture and memory systems. Knowledge and experience with game development systems. Conversant in PC and Console game development techniques and best practices. Lateral thinking and motivation to work independently and on a team as well as the ability to work directly with teams across multiple territories and time zones. Ability to create and maintain technical documentation from the beginning of a project through completion. Dedication, teamwork and professionalism. Great interpersonal skills. Desired Qualifications: Unreal engine experience highly desired 12+ years experience Previous experience in TD type roles Familiarity with current and next console SDKs, Steam, Epic Game Store. Expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Analytics, Mathematics, Physics, Graphics, or Audio a plus Experience working within Microsoft, Sony, EA, Take Two, and utilizing publishers best practice
