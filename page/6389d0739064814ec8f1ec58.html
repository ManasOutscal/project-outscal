Job Description

Are you looking for an opportunity to work on a brand-new title with a team of highly respected game developers? Are you a reliable, thoughtful collaborator who enjoys working with a highly creative and talented team and directing it to smooth and predictable outcomes? Our team at Stoic, 4-time BAFTA nominated independent video game company, is seeking a Sr. Producer. You will help us take the project from Production through launch. As a Sr. Producer your responsibility is to own and drive a part of the game and lead a cross-discipline development team through launch into a long-term live service. You are responsible for keeping the team focused and productive. This is a key role that involves a high level of interaction with team members, outsourcing partners, project, and studio leadership. The successful candidate will be passionate about helping our team achieve their creative vision. What You Will Do: Lead with enthusiasm, humility, empathy, honesty, and respect. Shape and drive production for a portion of our game and its live service. Work with directors and leads to establish clear goals, creative vision, and “what good looks like” for your team. Accurately forecast our trajectory, identify risks, and actively work with the team to address. Establish, drive, and communicate the long-term roadmap and schedule for the area of responsibility. Create highly efficient pipelines for areas of responsibility. Communicate with stakeholders to understand problems, solutions, and plans and bring pertinent information into planning discussions. Set quality targets and drive processes to ensure those targets are being met. Use data and analytics to inform decision making and ensure that decisions are made in a timely fashion and evaluated for efficacy. Need to Have: A minimum of 5 years of experience in a Production role in AAA games. 5+ years of experience developing and executing game project plans. 5+ years of experience leading, motivating and driving teams to success. Strong understanding of agile development values and practices. Strong logic and communication skills. Negotiation, change management, and conflict resolution skills. Ability to successfully partner within the studio. Sound decision-making based on data analysis, market knowledge, and personal judgement. Assertive and motivated team player with a bias for action. High degree of initiative, takes opportunities to make improvements. Ownership mentality: accountable, invested in outcomes. Humility and Integrity. Stoic has transitioned to a fully remote, forever remote workforce. We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, veteran status, or disability status.
