Job Description

Overview Splitgate is a free to play, fast-paced sci-fi multiplayer shooter that combines the strategic elements of portals with the explosive gameplay of a first person shooter. We quickly became a top trending game on PlayStation, XBOX, and Steam. In our first 6 days, we passed 1M downloads, and hit 2M within the first two weeks. We’re now at 15M+ downloads and growing. After receiving $100M Series A funding with a valuation of $1.5B, we are actively looking to grow our team with the best talent out there to continue the journey of making Splitgate a game that we and the millions of players in our gaming community are proud of. What We’re Looking for: We are looking for a talented Senior UI Artist who has great attention to detail and a passion for crafting beautiful UI experiences. In this role you will be responsible for iterating on concepts, delivering high-fidelity comps, and bringing visual designs to life in Unreal Engine. As a senior member of the team, you will help inspire fellow team members, take point on major features and ensure our tools support our workflow needs. Responsibilities: Craft and iterate on high-fidelity visual comps, prototypes, and polished designs for game features and HUD Create artwork, iconography, and rasterized assets that are high-quality and optimized for the game Work with UX designers to visualize various interface solutions Work with art leadership to achieve desired visual direction and ensure consistency Work in unreal engine (UMG) to bring designs to implementation with the engineering team, and push for improvements to workflow Collaborate with a variety of disciplines including art, design, engineering, audio, and production Qualifications 3+ years of experience working in interactive media with a focus on user interface design Game industry experience on at least 1 commercially shipped title Strong sense of composition, color, and working knowledge of graphic design fundamentals High proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, or other industry-standard design tools Good communication, interpersonal, organizational, and time management capabilities Strong problem-solving, analytical, strategic, and troubleshooting skills. Passion for games Love for visual design and awareness of trends future, current, and dated Desired Qualifications Ability to create logos, symbols, illustrative emblems, and icons Technical proficiency in Unreal Engine (UMG) Motion graphics experience Technical proficiency in scripting materials (shaders) Familiarity with accessibility best practices and designing with them in mind Comfortable designing for immersive end to end user experiences Demonstrated history crafting style guides and/or component style libraries
