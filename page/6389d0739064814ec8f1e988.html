Job Description

Splitgate is a free to play, fast-paced sci-fi multiplayer shooter that combines the strategic elements of portals with the explosive gameplay of a first person shooter. We quickly became a top trending game on PlayStation, XBOX, and Steam. In our first 6 days, we passed 1M downloads, and hit 2M within the first two weeks. We’re now at 15M+ downloads and growing. After receiving $100M Series A funding with a valuation of $1.5B, we are actively looking to grow our team with the best talent out there to continue the journey of making Splitgate a game that we and the millions of players in our gaming community are proud of. What We're Looking For: A passionate art leader: You love to take on difficult, seemingly impossible problems and dig in to find creative ways to solve them A hard worker: You love to put in the extra effort, because you enjoy your work and know the impact of finishing the tasks at hand Passion for gaming: You can recognize what separates exceptional games from the average ones. You understand the mindset of gamers which enables you to create and implement features that will further benefit the game Responsibilities: Share a passion to innovate, especially in the design and development of open world games Work with the team to develop and manage an animation vision and production plan capable of delivering upon the promise of the game Keep up to date with the bleeding edge of animation trends within video game development and evangelize ideas that will innovate both the player experience and the creative development of the product Participate in the hiring of the animation discipline Responsible for the career growth and mentorship of the animation discipline on the project Act as the key animation contact for the studio, representing the project and team to all external partners and stakeholders Develop an animation vision for gameplay that meets the ambitions of AAA quality game experiences Define and uphold animation principles to ensure the animation direction of the game is cohesive, inspiring, and represents the right balance between good looking motion and fluid player input Work side-by-side with all directors and leads to take a multi-discipline approach to creativity and problem solving Work closely with the Game Director and Creative Director to develop animation intentions that reinforce and celebrate the gameplay and player fantasy Ensure that the animation direction of the game starts with a live product foundation, capable of regular updates and malleable to key changes if necessary Qualifications: A portfolio of visionary, inspiring, industry-leading gameplay animation that demonstrates your direction and capacity to execute on it within a game At least 8 years of professional AAA game development experience At least two projects shipped in a leadership position At least one AAA action title shipped At least one product shipped during the current generation of games (PS4/XBOX ONE) Demonstrable, outstanding management and communication capabilities Proven ability to define and manage the animation direction and roadmap for large-scale projects Proven ability to to define and develop gameplay animation systems (3C, Combat, NPC, ...) A team player who knows how to get the best out of everyone on the project and not just within art Experience creating animation in and shipping a game using Unreal Engine 4 Deep-rooted knowledge of animation software like Motion Builder, 3DS Max, and Maya
