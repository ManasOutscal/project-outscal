Job Description

Who we are: Chartboost is the leading in-app monetization and programmatic advertising platform. We reach a global audience of over 700 million monthly active users and process over 90 billion monthly advertising auctions. The Chartboost SDK is one the most widely integrated mobile ad SDKs and through the Chartboost Exchange, Ad Network, DSP and other services, we empower mobile app developers to build businesses, while connecting advertisers to highly engaged audiences. We are currently looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Platform Team. Impact you will have: As a Senior Software Engineer, your main responsibility will be coding on: Our public dashboard that our clients, publishers, and advertisers, use to get the most from our ad tech platform The internal tools that our Business Operations and Support teams use on a daily basis to help drive value to our customers This will be done by: Paying attention to architecture and applying coding best practices (automated testing, clean code, code reviews, etc.) in order to keep a high-quality development process Deploying several times a day through our continuous integration process Following Agile principles, receiving timely feedback from Product and Business that will let you know about the impact of your work Who you are: Degree in CS, mathematics, physics or equivalent work experience Abstract thinking and analytical skills Many years of experience in backend development using object-oriented languages Experience in frontend development Experience working with unit testing frameworks on both backend and frontend Comfortable with DevOps/CI tools (Travis, Jenkins, Spinnaker, etc.) Comfortable working with Docker Good knowledge of development tools like Git The following experience would be a plus: Python, ideally with Flask or Django Frontend frameworks like React/Redux (preferred), VueJS, or Angular, ideally with TypeScript Worked with Domain-Driven Design Implemented and used monitoring tools in production (ES/Kibana, Sentry) Worked with cloud infrastructure - GCP and/or AWS Perks: Competitive salary + Bonus Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) - you will have a stake in the future success of Chartboost and Zynga Flexible schedule based on core hours and the possibility of working from home. Private health insurance Annual budget for your professional development Company-sponsored lunch Healthy snacks, coffee, and drinks in our cool office in the center of Barcelona - near subway and train stations English/Spanish lessons Gym membership Transportation reimbursement Team building events More about us: We are proud of the product we've built and appreciate the impact it has on other people's businesses and lives. We want to be surrounded by people who are always finding opportunities to try something new and grow. We love data and anything that helps drive intelligent decisions and always design with the user in mind. Sounds like a fit? Join us, and be part of the team that will change the future of mobile gaming!
