Job Description

Hidden Path Entertainment is seeking a Senior Systems Programmer for a AAA, third-person, open-world fantasy RPG that will take place inside the Dungeons & Dragons franchise using Unreal Engine 5. The ideal candidate will have excellent software architecture skills, and will have experience developing core systems for video games. Hidden Path Entertainment is based in the Pacific Time Zone. Employees work a 40 hour week with daily overlapping hours of 10am to 4pm Pacific time. Hidden Path is an inclusive, collaborative environment. You will be working with artists, designers, engineers, and other disciplines from all walks of life who have a variety of different perspectives. Being able to navigate and thrive in this kind of environment will be key to your success. This is a full-time remote position. Post COVID, this position has the option to convert to an on-site role located in Bellevue, WA. Responsibilities: Own the entirety of a feature, including the user interface Help design and implement the architecture for interconnected core gameplay systems Create tools and visualizers to help tune, maintain, and test newly created systems Document features so that others can use them and understand them Assist with bug fixing and polish Requirements: 6+ years in the game industry Shipped at least one game title Excellent knowledge of C++ Excellent coding design skills Solid debugging skills Self-motivated and self-directed Enjoy problem solving and iterating Excellent verbal and written communication skills Nice to Have: B.S. in computer science or computer engineering Experience with Unreal Engine 4 or 5, and Blueprints Experience working with PC and consoles Experience working with Wwise
