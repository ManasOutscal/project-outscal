Job Description

Are you looking to change the gaming industry? We are! TLM Partners is a world-class digital entertainment company dedicated to breaking down barriers in the video game industry. We publish captivating and original indie games that bring people together through our best-in-class cross-play technology, while our professional services arm and cloud-based, fully-remote studio ensure that talent and expertise are always where they are needed most. TLM is at the forefront of making games and game development more accessible than ever. Gaming together. Anytime. Anywhere. Any device.™ Position Summary: Senior Rendering Engineer (UE4) Full time, 100% remote Develop rendering code for video games in a software engineering capacity, for various platforms including consoles and PC Be a part of a groundbreaking new global game developer and publisher, with a permanently remote team spanning 19 countries and counting (live where you want) Competitive salary, excellent benefits, and stock options available for full-time employees Responsibilities Write, maintain, and modify high quality C++ code in Unreal Engine 4 to implement and enhance rendering techniques Analyze, optimize, and improve rendering pathways and content creation workflows. Diagnose and fix rendering bugs, and profile and optimize rendering code as necessary Work closely with artists and other engineers to meet visual and technical goals Use company-provided workstation hardware, console dev kits, and video game industry standard software tools such as Visual Studio and Perforce to perform your work Communicate with other developers, company leadership, and external partners, using online tools such as Slack, Zoom, and Google Meet Educate colleagues on implemented features and rendering technologies Learning is one of our core values, as such, mentor others within your team and/or across departments Adhere to coding standards and best practices Required Qualifications Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or equivalent combination of education and experience Expertise in current game rendering pipelines and techniques 5+ years of graphics programming experience 1+ shipped AAA video game title credit as a rendering engineer Excellent C/C++, multi-threading, low-level engine experience, and 3D math / linear algebra skills Experience with modern graphics APIs, GPU shaders, GPU hardware, and common performance bottlenecks Experience with PC and console rendering performance optimization tools such as PIX and Razor Desire and ability to work remotely from a home office Desired Qualifications Great interpersonal skills Unreal 4 rendering/development experience Familiarity with common game industry tools & middleware Familiarity with current and next generation console SDKs
