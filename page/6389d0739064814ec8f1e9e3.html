Job Description

If you’re searching for something more than just a workplace, if you want to be a co-creator rather than just another cog in the machine, if you want to go down in the history of the game industry working as a part of great productions – read this ad. Bloober Team S.A. is not only a leading horror producer, with strong financial ground beneath it, a clear strategy and perhaps most interestingly, a portfolio of pending productions. Most of all, it’s a place of business with a comfortable and open working atmosphere, and a great team working with passion soon to be found on the new generation of consoles. If you’re looking not only for a well-paid job, but also a great atmosphere and the opportunity to be remembered for your work – apply to this position: Animation Programmer Within this role you will be responsible for: creating animation blueprints for various in-game characters (movement, special abilities, IK/FK solvers) work closely with technical animators on implementing various gameplay driven animations integration of animation blueprint with other various gameplay elements creating c++ plugins to aid technical animators (content importing plugins, additions to UE4 animation system, etc.) Job requirements Feel free to apply if you have: vast knowledge of UE4 animation system (animation blueprints, IK/FK), excellent knowledge of C++ (in context of animation programming in UE4), knowledge about FK/IK solvers, matrices, etc., practical knowledge of current trends and solutions in animations programming, great teamwork skills, English skills that allow you to communicate on an everyday basis. It would also be nice if you: have knowledge about UE4 AI/behaviour trees, have knowledge about facial animation techniques, have familiarity with Autodesk Maya / Motion Builder (writing scripts etc.), are a fan of a horror genre (books, movies etc.). What do we have to offer: Earnings fitting to your skills and experience, possibility of long-term cooperation on current and upcoming projects, work in a creative environment, in a committed team of game enthusiasts, work in a modern, comfortable office, flexible working hours, days off, private healthcare, and working with new technologies.
