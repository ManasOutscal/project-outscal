Job Description

This is a US-based remote position. No phone calls or emails from external recruiters or recruiting agencies. We are seeking a Project Manager to help manage Game and Experience development. This is a pivotal role working across Art, Design, and Engineering with the aim of bringing clarity and transparency to successful project launches. Working with Production, you will play a key role in developing our projects as you apply your management experience to help our teams create the best product in the most efficient manner. Your passion for planning and delivery will be evident as you work cross-functionally to resolve bottlenecks, developing processes and pipelines as you go. Come join us and help bring mass market AR experiences to our growing portfolio of clients! RESPONSIBILITIES Collaborate closely with Engineering, Design, Art, and other stakeholders to create and maintain project plans Use JIRA to develop project milestones and deliverables, maintaining these on a daily basis to track development Run Daily Stand-ups and other meetings associated with the project cadence. Sprint planning, retrospectives, etc Manage conflicting requirements by having a clear understanding of the objectives and keeping these in focus Communication of project status on an ad hoc basis as well as through regularly produced reports REQUIREMENTS 2+ years as a Project Manager with at least 2 completed, delivered projects under your belt Demonstrable experience working with a mixture of Engineering and either Art or Design departments Experience with JIRA and Confluence Must be an effective communicator NICE TO HAVE Be Bold - Be unafraid to make process changes that lead to improvements Experience of games or other AR content PERKS & BENEFITS Fully Remote! (US Based) Unlimited PTO & Nine Paid Company Holidays Full Coverage Medical, Dental, Vision, and Life Insurance (99% for Employees, 25% for Dependents) Self-Contributing 401K Monthly Stipend to cover Work From Home costs We are building a diverse, empowered, and passionate team to redefine the standard for mixed reality today.
