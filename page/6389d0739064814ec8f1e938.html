Job Description

Zynga India is looking for a TECHNICAL ARTIST to join the team that brings to life one of the most beloved franchises, GOT Slots! The Technical Artist will be working with a team of Designers, UX, Concept Artists, Animators, Producers and Software Engineers to develop aesthetically appealing and optimal game content in our own advanced version of Cocos Engine. The Technical Artist acts as liaison between engineering and all disciplines, responsible for the planning, implementation, documentation and optimization of art setups to support our game’s animation and design needs. A key member who’s primary role is to support the team and the product and is expected to be proactive in their communication on a daily basis. and Git Insatiable thirst for technical challenges A passion for supporting your peers and empowering them to do their job effectively and efficiently Some proficiency with OO Programming languages art setups and processes Ability to adapt, learn quickly and work on a fast paced environment Proficient creating content for game engines like Cocos, Unity or Unreal Expertise in optimizing art
