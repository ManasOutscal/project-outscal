Job Description

As our Manager - Quality Assurance, you will be responsible for ensuring the quality of our products. Working with a highly motivated team, you will help build the most successful gaming platform for the web and the mobile, hosting games played by millions. You will maintain overall quality and ensure that users have an absolutely enjoyable experience. You will be responsible to innovate, implement and scale up our QA capabilities, ensuring we keep up with our tremendous growth. Responsibilities Ensure products are well tested in different environments using available methodologies and practices before they are signed off for release. Utilize test automation capabilities by identifying use cases to automate, create action plans, drive technology choices and implementation, build and manage test automation framework, and provide visibility to test automation results across the organization. Develop test plans, test cases, test design documents with good coverage and optimized testing approach. Creating and maintaining all QA related documentation throughout the process Provide guidance and best practices on load testing, benchmarking, and performance testing across products. Recommend and utilize tools, frameworks, methodologies, and practices to improve all aspects of testing. Drive improvements in testing processes. Document and review processes and train the team to ensure a superior Quality process. Lead and grow the Quality Assurance team into an effective and respected division with both manual and automation engineers, identify new talent, and manage and retain talent. Hire and Manage a team of Quality engineers & SDETs. Set and manage stakeholder expectations. Communicate and execute goals within timelines. Translate the company’s overall vision into a series of objectives, laying down the roadmap to achieve the objectives and executing the resulting plan in a structured and time-bound manner. Institute best QA practices to deliver successful, innovative products. Work closely with the Engineering, Product, and other teams, and be involved in conceptualizing and building new products from scratch. Requirements A bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related technical discipline, or equivalent practical experience. Overall 8+ years’ relevant experience, out of which at least 3+ years experience in consumer-facing software products with high levels of concurrency in gaming, real-time banking, or e-commerce. Proven leadership and managerial skills to manage QA teams, and the ability to lead, inspire and improve team members. At least 2 years of experience in managing a team of QAE and SDET. Proficiency in Java, Python or any other programming language. Should have experience in code review and handling PRs. Experience with building UI test frameworks from scratch using open source automation tools and technologies like Selenium, Appium, TestNG. Experience with building and maintaining an API test automation framework from scratch using open source technologies like Rest-Assured. Good understanding of REST APIs and a solid experience in testing APIs Experience in working in a distributed microservice environment Experience with defect tracking and project management tools like JIRA. Knowledge of different browsers and cross-platform operating systems Experience with Testing Mobile apps (Android and iOS). Good understanding of CI/CD tools and technologies. Excellent written and oral communication and organizational skills with the ability to work within a growing company with increasing needs Proven track record of ability to handle time-critical projects Knowledge of cloud platforms like AWS, Azure Ability to independently manage test environment deployments and handle issues around it Good in system design and solve for complex testing challenges Extensive experience testing non-functional requirements such as security, reliability, performance, maintainability, and scalability. Experience in load and performance testing software systems and products with high concurrency. Familiarity with microservice architecture, containers, and related technologies (Docker, Kubernetes, ECS). Should be comfortable working with Linux as an application server. Expertise in any database (e.g Oracle, MySql, SQL Server, No-SQL databases). Background in high-volume web applications, data mining, and multi-tiered transactional systems.
