Job Description

Voodoo is a tech company that creates mobile games and apps. With 6 billion downloads and over 300 million monthly active users, Voodoo is the #2 mobile publisher worldwide in terms of downloads after Google. The company is one of the most impressive examples of hypergrowth in the ecosystem, having raised over $1B and backed by Goldman Sachs, Tencent, and GBL. Deciding to expand its portfolio of products further, Voodoo has more recently announced that it will invest $200M in blockchain gaming studios, to lead the transformation of mobile gaming. Voodoo is now a team of over 700 employees worldwide, we’re looking for talented individuals from across the globe to come and entertain the world with us. Team Being part of our Live games team is an opportunity to work with experts from the gaming industry on long lifetime games, played worldwide, to take on new entrepreneurial projects. We are also exploring brand new and exciting genres in mobile gaming such as blockchain and hybrid-casual games. Role We are looking for a Data/Business Analyst who is deeply passionate about data-driven decisions, transforming raw data into clear actionable business insights, and who is constantly driving themselves and the team towards completing the next challenge. Profile At least 3 years of analytical work experience in the gaming, tech or a data-driven industry, with a proven track record working in close connection with product teams. Proficient with SQL and either R or Python, with a proven experience working with large and complex data sets. Good knowledge of statistical analysis and concepts used for industry-standard methods like AB testing or forecasting. Autonomous person with a strong analytical mind, problem-solving skills and strong attention to detail. Ability to understand a business problem, turn it into an analytics/statistics problem and solve it using adequate methods. Highly pragmatic mindset with the ability to quickly identify what needs to be focused on and what can be considered of lower priority. Capacity to be the pro-active owner of the entire data chain for your game(s) Benefits: competitive salary and other benefits according to the country you reside.
