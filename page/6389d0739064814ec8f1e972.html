Job Description

Player First Games is building best-in-class online game experiences for a passionate playerbase. Each member joining our team will have a significant impact on the direction of the game and the growth of the company. As the Senior 3D Character Artist, you will: Responsibilities Create content, including 3D modeling/sculpting, 2D texture map painting, material setup/UV layout – adhering to defined quality standards, conceptual designs, artistic style, and technical parameters Work closely with the Art Director, Art Leads, Character Artists, and Concept Artists to create characters who represent the conceptual designs and fit the overall aesthetic and artistic vision Show experience in character art pipelines, assembly, and asset composition Ensure character assets are of the highest artistic quality and uphold the integrity of the games design and artistic vision of the game as a whole Proactively communicates and identify issues, as well as help drive participation in discussions Facilitate requests or info needs regarding any aspect of the character art creation process as needed Actively improves skillset and abilities by keeping aware of industry trends and techniques, evaluating competitive products, learning new software packages, and traditional art methods Possess strong time management and prioritization skills. Entails collaborating with Production group to develop task lists to ensure assignments are progressing in alignment with schedules, milestones, and overall project goals Clearly communicates updates and properly escalate concerns to the appropriate team member(s) and manager(s) Skills Good communication, organizational, time management, and interpersonal skills Works under minimal direction to determine and develop an approach to solutions Experience Minimum of 5 years industry or related experience A Bachelor’s in fine arts or a relevant curriculum or equivalent skillset Very Experienced with Unreal Engine 4 or equivalent Demonstrates the ability to develop a comprehensive knowledge of the art production pipeline and all engine features, tools, and systems associated with the creation of art assets Proficient in the use of Maya, (or 3D modeling package) Zbrush, Substance Designer, and Painter Familiarity with video game product development including, but not limited to workflow, tools, lighting, high and low poly modeling, texture painting, particle systems, UV layout, in-game animation, material setup, layout, SFX, and engine parameters/optimizations Previous or next-gen video game platform experience Preferred Qualifications Has shipped an AAA console or PC title as a senior character artist Mobile / Console / PC Games experience Cinematic or television art production experience A love for video games, movies, and animation Skill in one or more related disciplines – illustration, lighting, staging, color, composition We offer Competitive compensation and benefits Shared success in the company World-class talent with experience on some of the industry’s biggest games, including God of War(™), League of Legends(™), Overwatch(™), and Call of Duty(™) A culture focused on player trust and player experience Please note: Our teams are working remotely during the current COVID-19 outbreak and until circumstances allow for a safe return. Must be authorized to work in the United States and available to work from our home office when circumstances allow. At Player First Games we design experiences from the player’s perspective first.
