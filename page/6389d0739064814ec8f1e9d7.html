Job Description

The Senior VFX Artist will independently produce and deliver a wide range of high-quality visual effects through any stage of the production process in respect of the project’s technical and artistic constraints. You will guide and mentor junior artists while being a known resource for VFX art and best practices. Essential Functions/Responsibilities Conceptualize, design, and create stunning in-game visual effects (including modeling, texturing, animating, lighting, shaders, etc.) using available tools in Unity Work collaboratively with game designers and engineers to understand gameplay requirements and to effectively communicate gameplay mechanics to the player within the constraints of the engine Work with the art team to define a consistent style for VFX and to ensure the quality of the assets for all aspects of the project Ensure that VFX assets are built efficiently and within game constraints and given timelines Provide creative and innovative solutions to technical art problems and/or restrictions Develop tools & refine pipelines for the art production process, including batching and automation Assist in scoping work for on time milestone deliveries Guide and mentor other team members Generate turnarounds, and material callouts to aid in asset creation Exemplify reliability, accountability, and professionalism in all work-related interactions Help guide or lead cross-disciplinary dialogs to drive features from initial concept to completion Lead by example, creating a strong sense of purpose and community, and promoting a culture of excellence Manage shared library of assets and documentation for VFX Partner with Art Director to drive best practices for VFX Skills and Abilities Strong artistic skills and understanding of traditional art and animation principles Ability to create fully optimized and hyper realistic in-game VFX for a wide range of system requirements Positive attitude with the ability to give and receive constructive feedback Excellent organization, communication, and attention to detail Able to manage multiple, competing tasks and be self-directed to meet deadlines in a quickly evolving environment Ability to write scripts and actions to create VFX Strong desire to iterate and improve on inefficient processes Education and Experience 3 years’ experience creating real-time special VFX for video games Experience creating VFX in Unity Experience working with Photoshop, 3D applications (Maya or 3D Studio Max) and particle editors, as well as AfterEffects or similar programs for pre-visualizing VFX Advanced knowledge of most game development tools and ability to master proprietary tools and Date Created: 5/28/21 development processes Experience with traditional 2D animated VFX Experience or knowledge of repository tools (Git, SVN…) Experience with JIRA or other project management software Position Type/Expected Hours of Work Our core hours are 10am to 6:00pm cst, which means you should be available for meetings etc. during this time.
