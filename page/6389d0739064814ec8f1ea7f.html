Job Description

As a Lead Sound Designer for Predecessor, you will work with producers, designers, artists, and engineers to deliver high-quality audio that engages and connects to the players. Omeda Studios is at an exciting stage where the Sound team is just starting to form, you’ll have a direct impact at an early stage company in creating a strong sound direction for the game. You will help determine the resource needs and coordinate various disciplines internally and externally. In This Role You Will Empower and support your team to deliver the highest audio quality possible by providing direction and leadership Help to set ambitious goals and improve by challenging ideas, form a positive relationship with the team to bring out the best results Collaborate with the leads from other areas to create a shared vision and roadmap for the game Participate in setting and ensuring performance metrics and quality bars Provide feedback to group audio reviews to make sure the team works meets the quality of standard required and is in line with the audio vision Build and encourage an open feedback culture ensuring a lean feedback cycle and strong ability to collaborate within the team. Don’t be afraid to get your hands dirty: record, edit, and design sound effects Build strong audio features, systems, and other areas using competitive industry techniques and tools What We're Looking For At least 2 years of experience in a leadership role working on an in-house project Worked on at least 2 AA+ PC/Console titles Expertise in the holistic game audio process (Sound Design, Voice Over, Music Implementation, Technology, Player Experience, etc.) Experience with sound design tools such as Pro Tools, Cubase, Reaper, Ableton, Waves plugins, etc, high level of implementation between exporting assets from DAW to the game engine Experience with voice over processing tools and techniques Experience with audio middleware Wwise, Fmod Experience within the Unreal Engine, creating blueprints or similar visual scripting Organisation skills, autonomous along with the ability to anticipate risks for the audio pipeline Nice To Haves Experience with MOBAs or shooter games Background in music theory or music production
