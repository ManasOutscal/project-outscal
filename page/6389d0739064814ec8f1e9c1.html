Job Description

Deep Silver Volition has been creating wildly original and successful video games for over 25 years, including the fan-favorite Saints Row series. Our studio has prospered by making fun, innovative games no one else can make, while focusing on work-life balance and fostering a culture of collaboration, continuous growth, and learning.   We're looking for a talented and creative Senior Character Concept Artist to help define iconic, visually compelling characters for our future titles. Our games are well-known for pushing the limits of character customization, and the Character Concept Artists are critical to this mission. You'll help our team achieve the vision of our games by creating compelling and original character designs with a mind-blowing array of customization options, collaborating across Art, Design, and Programming to help the team achieve their aesthetic vision. This is a remote-friendly role. We’re a hybrid work environment, headquartered in Champaign, IL but with employees working permanently remotely across the U.S.  (We’re currently unable to hire in CA & NY, unfortunately.) What you’ll do as a Senior Character Concept Artist at Deep Silver Volition:  Inspire the team by exploring and defining the setting, tone and mood of the game through richly detailed and inspiring concept art. Curate references, develop mood-boards, and illustrate original concepts for hyper-realistic Character designs from visual and written briefs. Create concept art as exemplars that develop a vision and set the standard for future concepts by other artists. Use your knowledge of anatomy and fashion to build a library of compelling and unique character options Provide timely & detailed feedback for the concept art team and outsourcing vendors. Help the Art Director develop and organize the character art style guide. Proactively engage with leadership and peers across departments to align on high-level priorities and maintain strong lines of communication. Focus on the big picture: understand when details matter, and when broad strokes are more important.  What we’re looking for:  5+ years' experience in the game industry working in AAA game development as a Character Concept Artist. An outstanding portfolio that showcases your artistic ability and imagination. Excellent foundational art skills with a strong grasp of composition, color theory, perspective, character anatomy, gesture, style, and graphic design. Ability to iterate quickly via thumbnails/sketches/roughs, and to take a concept all the way to a rendered color final as needed. Extensive experience in 2D content creation tools. Strong communication skills, with a thoughtful collaboration style. Ability to work with a library of assets to build visually interesting new Concepts from existing assets. A keen eye for developing a visual mood as it relates to storytelling. Pluses:  Familiarity with procedural content authoring tools (Substance Designer, Zbrush, Marvelous designer).  Basic 3D art skills. Basic clothing design knowledge. Sample Work Required:  Candidates must submit an online portfolio to be considered. Some of our benefits: Subsidized medical/dental/vision insurance Company-paid life, short-term, & long-term disability insurance Generous paid time off (including parental leave and an annual company shutdown between Christmas & New Year's) 401k with company contribution Annual learning & development budget + access to GDC Vault + LinkedIn Learning Paid subscriptions to Headspace, Doordash, Sworkit, and other wellness apps At Deep Silver Volition we believe in fostering an open, collaborative, and diverse environment, and we are proud to be an equal opportunity employer. We will consider all qualified applicants without attention to race, color, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, genetic information, disability, age, veteran status, or military status.
