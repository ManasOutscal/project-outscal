Job Description

As a Gameplay Engineer you will develop powerful gameplay systems for the Call of Duty franchise. A Gameplay Engineer should be able to rapidly prototype ideas and be capable of quickly turning a design specification from paper into a usable game system for further iteration. A successful Gameplay Engineer will have an “eye” for gameplay and an interest in understanding how to deliver exciting features that millions of fans are going to love. Priorities can often change in a fast-paced environment like ours, so this role includes, but is not limited to, the following responsibilities: Contribute to the design of our gameplay systems Develop, modify, and debug gameplay software in script and C/C++ Deploy, optimize and test gameplay software on PC and console platforms Collaborate with other internal departments (art, design, audio, QA, etc.) Document software and features for internal and external teams Participate in the code review process Player Profile: We love hearing from anyone who is enthusiastic about changing the games industry.
