Job Description

T2 Security Operations Center is looking to add a level one SOC Analyst to our real time coverage Bangalore SOC team. This position plays a crucial role in monitoring and responding real time to incidents that pose a risk to the T2 and partner label environments. As part of a highly available Security Operation Center (SOC), the analyst primary responsibilities will be to monitor and process cyber security events as they occur and to ensure our security suite of tools are functioning as designed. The analyst is expected to provide feedback and participate in the continuous improvement of the SOC. Role Responsibilities Monitor security suite of tools for alerts and events that pose a risk to the T2 and partner label environments Follow escalation and playbook processes efficiently for events classified as critical or higher Resolve or escalate XSOAR playbook failures, loss of SPLUNK or other critical data ingest feeds or other network and end-point sensor loss Monitor T2 Phishing Mailbox and process, respond or escalate following SOP and playbook workflows Monitor XSOAR generated tickets and take appropriate action per designated playbook guidance Develop and follow detailed operational processes and procedures to appropriately analyze, escalate and assist in remediation of information security-related incidents Effectively follow and enhance cross team communication, collaboration, and incident response processes Leverage automation to improve workflow quality and efficiency as appropriate Provide host and network forensic support to acquire system, network and situational artifacts during active security incident response investigations Understand and perform malware analysis during event and incident response investigations Must be willing to work various shifts and on-call availability Qualifications 1-3 years' experience as a Security /Network Analyst Knowledge of various security methodologies and processes, and technical security solutions (firewall, packet analysis, SIEM and intrusion detection systems) Ability to analyze endpoint, network, and application logs Knowledge of common Internet protocols and applications Sound problem resolution, judgment, negotiating, and decision-making skills Strong work ethic and commitment to accomplish assigned tasks Tenacity & Curiosity Splunk, XSOAR experience Scripting experience in Python or PowerShell a huge plus Bachelor's degree in a related field or equivalent demonstrated experience and knowledge Sec+, CYSA, GCIA, GCIH, GMON or related training and certifications What We Offer You: Great Company Culture.
