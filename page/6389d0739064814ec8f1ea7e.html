Job Description

In This Role You Will Take ownership and design, prototype, and implement core gameplay UI, HUD, and game menus that are attractive, intuitive, and ergonomic within a multiplatform universe Work closely with Game Designers, Tech Artists, and Engineers to craft an experience that delivers on player aspirations and facilitates deep immersive gameplay Build a deep understanding of competitors in the market to inform designs that will meet player expectations and create a best-in-class experience Contribute, innovate, and develop original ideas on all aspects of the game What We're Looking For An experienced UI Designer that worked on implementing UI widgets and features in Unreal or similar game engines Ability to give and receive constructive feedback when interacting with other team members, keeping the focus on making the best game possible Strong expertise in design fundamentals and UI/UX design trends Good knowledge of the pipeline for motion design, UI animation, and VFX A strong portfolio showcasing your design projects and UI samples Technical knowledge of UE4 and UMG is a plus Nice to Have Experience designing UI for cross-platform is a plus (PC/Console) Interest and understanding of F2P/GaaS A passion for Games and MOBAs
