Job Description

Lead the way in UX Design, for all of Team17’s games! Team17 develops and releases some of the world’s best loved games. We are uniquely positioned thanks to our broad and diverse games label catalogue, which plays host to an exciting mix of both internally and externally developed titles. We have our own multi-million selling IP, we’re always exploring interesting new concepts, and we love collaborating and supporting our development partners in bringing games to life. We are looking for a Lead UX Designer to join our growing team. This is a fantastic opportunity to get hands on, but also to help build and grow a dedicated UX Design team, here at Team17. You will work closely with our dedicated team of Game Designers, Programmers, Artists and User Researchers, advocating for the player throughout the development process. You will have the ability to directly influence the UX for all of our titles, as well as lead through defining processes and best practices. Some of the things you will do: Lead the way on defining processes for developing all UX related features of our internally developed titles, as well as how we support our third-party development partners on the games label from a UX point of view Help build a dedicated UX team through recruitment - and mentor, train and grow that team over time You will be providing feedback and suggesting improvements to game UX across a variety of development teams, aiming to improve the quality of our titles in all things related to UX Advocating for the player during development and collaborating with the User Research team to analyse research and find solutions to problems and challenges, coupled with advocating for accessibility in our games Ensure visual consistency and high quality in our User Interfaces, working with UI artists to create and design systems, whilst being hands-on alongside designers and programmers to help implement interfaces in our games using popular engines You will get hands on, designing directly through creating wireframes, user flows and other artefacts, and by defining features and providing solutions Some of the ways you will do it: Through your love of gaming and up to date latest knowledge of industry trends, tools techniques and technology, setting a high standard of work Through your experience of creating clear documentation, your ability to design iteratively and utilising your knowledge of to present and deliver solutions to a variety of audiences Using your experience of design and prototyping tools (e.g., Figma) to best support the games that you work on Call on your leadership skills to recruit, mentor, train and grow a dedicated UX Design team You will be a keen communicator and collaborator with a strong understanding of the digital landscape, especially when it comes to games!
