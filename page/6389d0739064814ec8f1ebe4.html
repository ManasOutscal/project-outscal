Job Description

Lumos Labs is the industry leader in brain training. Since the launch of Lumosity in 2007, more than 100 million users have trained their cognitive skills by playing over 7 billion training games. We believe in helping people keep their brains challenged through playful, meaningful experiences grounded in science. From neuroscience to art, we combine diverse disciplines to create a brain training program that is both engaging and accessible — making cognitive research available to everyone. We draw upon scientific research, user-friendly design, and engagement with our user base to continuously improve our product. Through the Human Cognition Project, we collaborate with over 100 leading researchers and clinicians from institutions around the world. We’re committed to building upon our brand to become world leaders in digital health and medicine. WE'RE LOOKING FOR We are looking for a Senior Infrastructure Engineer to join our Core Services team who will oversee code releases and assist with technical architecture, combining an understanding of security, engineering, and coding. Help improve the development team's productivity by creating and implementing new tools and analyzing data to improve existing ones. You are a curious engineer that constantly thinks about improvements in observability, monitoring, and scaling aspects of the infrastructure. We have applications running on Kubernetes, ECS, and Serverless. We’re constantly looking to build out more tools and processes to support our user-facing, data engineering, and data science teams. YOU WILL Develop and maintain all aspects of our operational infrastructure including codifying everything via CDK (Javascript/Typescript). Create next-gen infrastructure supporting external and internal applications. Improve Observability and Monitoring to preemptively help scale our systems. Build tools to reduce occurrences of errors and improve customer experience. Employ preventive measures to maintain high availability of services. YOU HAVE 4-6 years experience in site operations. Strong software engineering, database, system administration, and infrastructure management skills. Managed operations and infrastructure for a large-scale website. Good knowledge of writing code in Python, Typescript, or Javascript. Familiarity with the following technologies: AWS, Infrastructure as Code, Container Orchestration, Serverless, Continuous Delivery/Deployment, and Version Control. Database management in MySQL, PostgreSQL, DynamoDB, and overall Linux knowledge. Enthusiasm to work in small teams helping development teams be more self-efficient and self-serviced. Bonus Qualifications Experience building infrastructure using AWS CDK. Proficient with writing code in Typescript. Worked in a regulated industry and experienced securing infrastructure per HIPAA or HITECH guidelines. Generous company-sponsored medical, dental, and vision plans (options for individual, spouse, and/or domestic partner, and family coverage). Continuous learning budget for conferences, professional and skill development – think Udemy Courses, Coursera and books. Monthly fitness, wellness, internet, co-working, and cell phone reimbursements.
