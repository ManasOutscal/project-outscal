Job Description

At Arkadium our purpose is to have fun while creating fun - and that’s exactly what we’ve been doing for 20 years. We’re the creators behind the world’s most-played games. From the Solitaire that came on your Windows machine, to hundreds of other games you’ve come to know and love - you can find our games on Arkadium.com, iOS, Android, and lots of other big sites worldwide. As a proud Evergreen business our values of Fierce Drive, Positive Energy and Living Full Lives are the driving force behind how we run and the choices we make. We’re privately held (aka stable and reasonable about growth), founder-led (aka passionate about our craft) and we consistently rank as a “Best Place to Work” (aka not your typical games company). Interested in becoming an Arkadian? We’d love to connect with you. Overview This Senior Full-Stack Engineer will work on the backend platform that enables our games to provide functionality between the web, mobile apps, and our databases. You will design, build, test, and support API endpoints to enable user authentication, inventory management, player history, purchases, subscriptions, and in-game reward mechanics. This engineer will develop features for internal admin websites and content management systems allowing product owners and content managers complete control over the user community and game customizations. We develop systems that engage millions of users a day. This role will join an all-star team including a project manager, a technical engineering manager, technical lead engineer, a senior software engineer, and a dedicated QA engineer. Each programmer on the team has 10 years or more of web engineering experience. You will be directly involved in projects that engage with key company stakeholders including: the CEO, President, Product Owners, Creative, Marketing, Data, and Front End developer teams. If you are 5-9 years professionally experienced and passionately interested in both frontend and backend development and get hyped about React, Node, and TypeScript. This is your golden opportunity to make a great contribution in a successful and growing company competing in the gaming industry. This role is open to candidates based in Portugal and will remain remote for the time being. For those in Portugal, business hours are 9-18 WEST (UTC+1). Responsibilities Development of web applications and websites of varying degrees of complexity, from feature rich single page applications to internal libraries used by other developer teams. Development of web services and well-structured API's. Design of data base structures and schemas. Collaborating with front-end developers on the integration of UX elements and backend service connected functionality. Developing high-performance applications by writing testable, reusable, and efficient code. Keeping informed of advancements in the field of Node.js development. Leading Architectural decisions which impact product teams, communicating to stakeholders risks and opportunities with implementation details. Requirements 5-9 years professionally experienced and passionately interested in both frontend and backend development (React, Node, and TypeScript). Strong Knowledge of traditional topics in Computer Science such as: data structures, optimization, and the client-server model. Three or more years of backend/frontend development experience with official work in companies and teams. Backend: TypeScript, Node, Express. Frontend: TypeScript, React. Work experience building RESTful API's. Work experience with PostgreSQL. Work experience with Web Sockets. Work experience with code organization, MVC, atomic principles, OO and functional programming. Integrations between third party libraries and API's such as authentication and transaction systems. Deep understanding of native JS, HTTP Methods, HTTP Codes, Browser DOM, and Virtual DOM. ES6, HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Webpack, NPM. All applicants must be legally authorized / have the necessary visa to work in Portugal Benefits Competitive compensation with structured performance and career development check-ins Annual employee profit sharing program (because we're all it in together) Meaningful supplemental health insurance (medical, dental & vision plans) Meal allowance Additional budget for conferences you are interested in Personal development budget to fuel your passions and desire to learn (Arkadians are taking classes in not only Excel and leadership, but also interior design and even swimming!) Time off from Christmas Eve through New Year's Day so we can all enjoy the holidays A unique culture of transparency, true teamwork and fun
