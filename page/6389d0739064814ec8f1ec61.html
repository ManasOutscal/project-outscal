Job Description

BTV Interactive is seeking an experienced and dynamic Senior Producer to join our growing mobile games team. As our ideal candidate you understand the ever-changing mobile, free-to-play landscape and how to manage multiple projects simultaneously. You’re a natural born problem solver keen to create the highest quality gaming experiences for our target audiences. Key Responsibilities: Work with the Head of Interactive to plan project scope and the milestone schedules for each BBTV Interactive game, also preparing the relevant statement of work for the game developer. Ensure projects are completed on time, on budget and to quality targets, having the tough conversations necessary to rebalance those goals effectively. Monitor KPI performance for released games and work with the Interactive team and the Senior Producer to continually update and add features to improve KPIs. Analyze the successes and failures of our games to effectively collaborate with the Head of Interactive on how to plan the next round of releases. Collaborate with other members of our production team to ensure product roadmaps are accurate and aligned with our department goals. Run and analyze play tests of BBTV’s games to continually improve them. Assist the Head of Interactive with recruiting new consultants as necessary to ensure all projects are sufficiently resourced to achieve their respective deadlines. Remove roadblocks for game designers and developers to ensure a smooth and efficient development process. Effectively communicate the status of each game Head of Interactive, including progress against time and quality targets and any relevant KPI risks. Build and improve upon internal processes as necessary to facilitate team growth and efficiency. Key Skills and Requirements: 5+ years of full-cycle experience in the mobile game industry in a producer role including managing relationships with external developers and/or IP holders. Able to multitask a number of games simultaneously at different stages of development. Strong analytical and reporting capabilities. Outstanding verbal and written communication skills with a proven track record of communicating effectively with stakeholders at all levels. A charismatic self-starter with a positive attitude and a desire to drive initiatives across the finish line. Comfortable with term sheets and legal agreements. Resourceful in making decisions in a timely manner, with the ability to empower our team and partners to troubleshoot and problem solve.
