Job Description

Job Summary Flux is looking for a talented Cinematic Technical Artist to be part of our arts team during an exciting project we are developing! We are growing our team to more one big consoles and PC production and we need a passionate and committed artist to guarantee our quality terms, be responsible for the cinematic tools. If you are passionate about technical arts and cinematic, you could be the one! Join us to develop best in class user experiences for an innovative game with a cool and famous IP. This contract is for 06 months of work with renewal possibility. Remuneration is negotiable based on portfolio and experience. This job is for remote work. Responsibilities And Duties Translating the narratives, immersing them in the gaming experience. Collaborate with all the development teams and orchestrate all the factors required for the work. Always be up to date and promoting innovations within time. Acting with creativity not only for the content of cinematic, but also the means to achieve a good result overcoming technical and artistic challenges. Actively use communication channels to present your progress and be present in the development. Use software in ways that act as an extension of your creativity and amplify its capabilities. Develop together with your team, focusing on activities to contribute in the best way to the project. Communicate your progress across channels daily and be mindful of team communication. Qualifications And Skills Experience working with timeline. Basic knowledge of photography. Enjoy working in a team. Willing to learn and always seek new knowledge. Be communicative and proactive. Bonus Qualifications Experience working in Unity. Experience working with games or audiovisual. Strong English skills.
