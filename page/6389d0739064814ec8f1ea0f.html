Job Description

Magic Media is a pioneering media, entertainment and tech group powered by creativity and innovation. We have a physical presence in 13 countries and expertize in the areas of art, animation, cybersecurity, game development, software development, VFX and video production amongst others. We work with leading developers and publishers within the games and tech industry, providing support and solutions designed around individual needs and carried out by global experts. We produce our industry-leading results by constantly staying at the forefront of modern technology and cutting-edge processes and practices. The best of Magic Media’s teams are known for their strong initiative, not just in taking care of their day-to-day responsibilities but in helping identify ways to build, grow, and improve. We are looking for a Senior 3D Environment Artist to join our team on a variety of projects. The candidate should have extensive and demonstrable experience creating and dressing any kind of 3D space in different styles and pipelines specifically among the videogame industry. As a Senior 3D environment artist, you will be responsible for the creation of different kinds of props and prepare and dress 3D spaces. This includes modelling the mesh, texturizing and preparing the assets to be delivered under developers or tech arts demands. As a senior it could be required to define the pipelines, solve and fix issues coming from outside and assist leading the juniors and completing estimations or other documentation. Skills Extensive knowledge of the standard 3D packages, 3Ds max, Maya, Blender… Proficient skills in illumination and RT rendering Advanced knowledge and experience in engines like unity and unreal Capability to achieve expected visual goals and style without full defined blueprints Good understanding of which style and visuals are expected in each task or project. Ability of art creation pipelines, asset exporting, and process automation workflows Excellent communication skills Reporting and documentation skills Requirements +5 years experience and involved in various game developments A passion for video games Capability to lead or atleast assist workmates. Ability to answer and help workmates regarding specific 3D tasks Experience in various standard 3D packages creating environments Animation, VFX and lighting basic skills Desirable pluses Tech art background Procedural environment insights or basic knowledge Tech Experience with Unreal or Unity assets integration or illumination Non-RT Rendering skills Benefits Permanently remote position An opportunity to experience different pipelines and styles and improve your skills by applying them to a diverse variety of engaging projects. Be part of an international Group with offices worldwide Career growth and development Working closely with a team of like-minded people in a fast-paced, multicultural environment Ongoing training and professional self-improvement opportunities Flexible working hours An inclusive culture and open communication
