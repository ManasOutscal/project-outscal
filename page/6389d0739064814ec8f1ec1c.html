Job Description

At Arkadium our purpose is to have fun while creating fun - and that’s exactly what we’ve been doing for 20 years. We’re the creators behind the world’s most-played games. From the Solitaire that came on your Windows machine, to hundreds of other games you’ve come to know and love - you can find our games on Arkadium.com, iOS, Android, and lots of other big sites worldwide. As a proud Evergreen business our values of Fierce Drive, Positive Energy and Living Full Lives are the driving force behind how we run and the choices we make. We’re privately held (aka stable and reasonable about growth), founder-led (aka passionate about our craft) and we consistently rank as a “Best Place to Work” (aka not your typical games company). Interested in working with us? We’d love to hear from you. Overview Arkadium seeks a talented Dev Ops Engineer to join our team. In this role, you'll have the opportunity to collaborate with multiple teams (developers, analysts and data engineers) and make some real impact to the business. If you enjoy constantly improving and automating infrastructure so teams can deliver services quickly, then we'd love to hear from you! Responsibilities Improve CI/CD pipelines Automate and template deployments Reduce tech debt on fault tolerance, scalability and security requirements Maintain applications/pipelines dependencies up-to-date Pilot new technologies and services Oversee incident eradication together with product teams and operations (including root cause analysis, postmortems Act as a bridge between development and operations Design and implement build/release pipelines Automate deployments (Helm charts, Ansible playbooks); Work with a dynamically changing infrastructure in the environment of IT Pro – developers, analytics, data engineers and QA Requirements Experienced with GIT, CI/CD, CLI tools Familiar with containerized environments/Kubernetes troubleshooting Possesses basic programming skills Strong knowledge of computer networks and web applications Experience automating workflows An advantage if you are familiar with the following Tech: Cloud environments (applications, storages – we’re most on Azure, some services – GCP/AWS) Azure DevOps Web servers (nginx or any other) IaC (Terraform) In addition to scripting languages – node.js/TypeScript Web app debugging (Chrome DevTools, cURL); Containers and orchestrators (Docker, Azure Kubernetes Services) Benefits Competitive compensation with structured performance and career development check-ins Meaningful supplemental health insurance (medical, dental & vision plans) Meal allowance Additional budget for conferences you are interested in Personal development budget to fuel your passions and desire to learn (Arkadians are taking classes in not only Excel and leadership, but also interior design and even swimming!) Time off from Christmas Eve through New Year's Day so we can all enjoy the holidays A unique culture of transparency, true teamwork and fun Summer Fridays: 4 Fridays off throughout the Summer to have more fun in the sun
