Job Description

DigitalFish has an immediate need for an Unity Engineer - AR/VR with professional experience in interactive games, or interactive visualizations of engineering data in XR. As a Unity Engineer - AR/VR with DigitalFish, you will develop next-generation technologies that define the future of digital-media creation and consumption for millions of users. DigitalFish has long been a trusted partner of leading digital media companies, and we are at the center of their efforts to build a new generation of platforms and experiences. Current and past customers include Apple, Google, Meta, Disney, DreamWorks, Activision, Technicolor, ESPN, LEGO, NASA and many others. Ideal candidates will have 3-5 years relevant production work experience, but if you think you’re right for this position, contact us; we’ll listen. Responsibilities Write software to ingest, process and visualize captured and/or simulated scene data Design and implement visualization tools to support troubleshooting and analysis of the data capture and simulation systems Take ownership of technical issues and work to resolve them Identify and reduce inefficiencies in the pipeline and tools Evaluate and recommend third-party software solutions Minimum Requirements BS/BA in Computer Science or related engineering field Minimum 2 years experience using Unity in a commercial application 3+ years of experience engineering in C# or C++ 1+ year experience engineering in Qt with either C++ or Python Good understanding of linear algebra, image processing, and 3D graphics concepts Strong general programming skills Familiarity with Agile/Scrum development process Self-motivated with strong communications skills and able to work both in a team and independently Proof of eligibility to work in the United States Compensation and Benefits Competitive salary plus equity per qualifications Full benefits with excellent health plan Ability to influence product and features in a cutting edge technology Work with a well-respected, creative and talented group of experts in graphics and animation Location Our team is fully remote, working effectively together in real time. The ability to work on West Coast time zone is preferred. Additional Info Principals only, recruiting firms please do not contact us. We are an equal opportunity employer and are hiring immediately. DigitalFish is not able to sponsor immigration petitions for this role at this time. All applicants must have the right to work in the U.S. We look forward to hearing from you!
