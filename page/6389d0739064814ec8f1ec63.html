Job Description

EUROPE · FULLY REMOTE Omeda Studios are a remote-first game studio working on our first title Predecessor, a fast-paced third-person MOBA for PC and consoles, due for release in 2022. Join a rapidly growing game studio as we scale up to support and deliver content for a game designed to be played for years to come. As a studio founded by the community, developing games that players will love is truly at the heart of what we do. At Omeda Studios, we believe that community feedback is integral to the development of games that players will not want to put down. Having recently closed a $20m Series A funding round, we've populated the studio with an exciting mix of industry veterans and community talent and we want you to be a part of shaping and executing our vision and the challenges that await us. And here's where you come in! We need an experienced Publishing Producer like you to help us successfully develop and release new and exciting cross-play content. In This Role You Will Take an active role in the day-to-day development of Predecessor by being responsible for features or parts of the team Work closely with other Producers and the Leadership team to drive the build and distribution of various types of releases Help to create, manage and coordinate release schedules for upcoming cross-play content that champion first-party submissions (SONY, Microsoft) Provide advice and guidance for age ratings, first-party policies, certification requirements, and best practices Perform administrative services for access to first-party development documentation, tools, and access Keep a good relationship with our first-party partners by being their go-to person for all questions What We're Looking For At least 3 years experience in a Production role where you have shipped at least one game within a development studio Experience managing the release of games content across Sony and Microsoft Demonstrated ability to juggle feedback, priorities, and deadlines in a fast-paced environment Driven and proactive nature mixed with a can-do attitude Excellent written and verbal english communication skills Experienced with Jira / Confluence Nice to Have Experience working on a cross-play games (PC + Consoles) Experience working on a live-ops title with regular updates and patches A genuine passion for video games (MOBAs are a plus) Salary band for this role is £45k-£60k depending on experience. Benefits Global, remote team 33 days holiday inclusive of bank holidays EMI Stock Options Healthcare More to come!
