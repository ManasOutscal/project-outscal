Job Description

Are you looking for an opportunity to work with a growing team on an exciting new project? Do you consider yourself well-versed in the ways and means of durable services, platform integrations, seamless deployments, and streamlined live operations? As a Backend Engineer at Stoic, you will have the opportunity to work with the engineering team to develop the service backend for an exciting and ambitious new game. You will be responsible for selecting technology as well as integrating with existing platforms. You will be able to take the state-of-the-art to the next level, for highly available and maintainable game services. What You Will Do Gather requirements and plan out a reasonable work schedule. Collaborate with a wide range of disciplines in a respectful and empathetic manner. Design, implement, test, and maintain robust backend services to power new game features. Build backend infrastructure that is stable, scalable, and testable. Work with client engineers to integrate new backend APIs with the game. Build out web-based tooling for a variety of audiences to interact with backend systems. Need to Have Experience developing and operating web microservices at scale Strong skills in an object-oriented language (we use C#) Significant experience with at least one major cloud providers (we use Azure) Excellent verbal and written communication skills Nice to Have Server-authoritative online game architecture and best practices Cloud containerization tooling (we use Kubernetes, Docker) Infrastructure as Code tooling (we use Terraform) CI/CD systems (we use Azure DevOps) Frontend web development technologies (JavaScript, HTML, CSS, etc) Third-party game platform tooling (PlayFab, Xbox, Commerce systems) C++ and the Unreal engine Security and encryption Wide variety of database technologies (SQL, NoSQL) Stoic has transitioned to a fully remote, forever remote workforce.
