Job Description

Job Summary Flux is looking for a talented, very creative and inspiring Game Designer also with technical skills to work on several key features in an exciting project we are developing! We are growing our team to our next big consoles and PC production and we need a passionate and committed Game Designer to make the game feel great and fun! We are open to any level of seniority and experience. If you are passionate about Game Design, you could be the one! Join us to develop best in class user experiences for an innovative game with a cool and well-known IP. Remuneration is negotiable. This job is for remote work Responsibilities And Duties Work closely with product owners, directors, and leads to push project quality and ensure that game design is in line with the project's creative vision, technical guidelines and standards. Participate in the constant improvement of work methods and pipelines. Instruct and assist in the training and day-to-day of other game designers on the team. Design combat systems and adjacent structures to ensure a fun game and in keeping with the project's overall vision. Qualifications And Skills You have a understanding of all aspects of design including: feedback, controls, balance, narrative, game-feel, retention, usability, on-boarding. You boldly embrace change and are always willing to grow and learn new things. You are a team player with a positive attitude and superior motivation to create high quality content. Verbal and written English skills. Bonus Qualifications Experience in Combat Design. Experience in Programming Logic or C#. Experience in Game Economy. Experience in Game UI/UX. Shipped at least one Console title. Strong verbal and written Portuguese skills; Area Design Role Game designer Locations Remote Work Remote status Fully Remote
