Job Description

PeopleFun™ is the award-winning creator of mobile games for players across the globe. We are part of the AppLovin™ studios family, a publicly traded company, with world-class projects, resources, and infrastructure. PeopleFun™ is a Dallas-based game studio founded and maintained by game industry veterans, where game developers thrive on collaboration, creativity, teamwork and fun. Our games are played by over 25 million players each month, and we have a number of exciting mid-core and casual games in development. Our mission is twofold: excellence in creativity and data-driven product innovation, and to be a best-in-class workplace providing unique opportunities to learn and exercise curiosity. Candidates who reside or will reside in the following locations are eligible for this role: California, Nevada, Oregon, Texas, Colorado, Washington State, Ohio, North Carolina, Florida, Pennsylvania, Washington DC, Michigan, New York, Mississippi, Georgia, Maryland, Iowa or Toronto. PeopleFun is seeking a highly skilled .NET Server Engineer to join our backend engineering team. Working on the game team you will be integral to the planning and implementation of a robust server architecture to propel the product into live service and beyond. As an expert in your craft, you have experience designing and architecting C# server code, as well as the ability to maintain and build upon existing features and code. Professional experience of GCP (Cloud Run or GKE) architecture planning and development is desirable. You will preferably have online and mobile gaming development experience, along with strong interpersonal skills and a passion for video games. Responsibilities: Design and implement well-engineered, secure, reliable, maintainable, and bug-free API’s, applications and systems Select suitable frameworks and programming languages for the task Work closely with the Lead Developer and server team to develop and improve client, server, and database architecture Backend and database performance assessment, support, and optimization Design and develop solutions addressing reliability, scalability and security Implement industry best practices from an architectural and engineering standpoint Integrate existing client/server systems with 3rd party platforms Requirements: 5+ years engineering experience developing RESTful API’s and backend servers to support client applications, especially mobile applications Expert in C# .NET core Knowledge and experience of ASP.NET Experience coding against large data systems Familiarity with Google cloud-based services or AWS and engineering development including CI/CD practices Experience working with k8s and containerized applications (eg. Docker)A champion of code quality and a proven mentor Desired Qualities: Professional experience with web and cloud technologies such as GCP, AWS or similar services Experience working with Anthos Service Mesh Experience in deploying, operating, and scaling dedicated game servers for session-based, multiplayer games Experience developing, maintaining and running live ops for world-scale games Directly involved with developing, maintaining, shipping, and supporting full development cycle of multiple client/server-based games Benefits Competitive compensation package Performance bonuses 401K with 3% employer matching Family friendly culture Flex PTO policy Medical/Dental/Vision insurance On-Site Gym and free Yoga classes $1,500 annual budget for career development & education $1,000 annual game device and IAP budget Free drinks & snacks, catered lunch on Fridays Happy hours, social events and more PeopleFun is an equal opportunity employer.
