Job Description

Crystal Dynamics is constantly seeking the best gaming talent worldwide to add to our 30-plus year legacy of AAAA game development creating new and iconic franchises. We are committed to making great games with great people and having an awesome time living the dream while doing it. Benefits for our full-time positions include, but are not limited to, a competitive regional salary, 100% employer-paid medical, dental, and vision insurance options, professional development reimbursement perk, flexible hybrid on-site/off-site work opportunities, 12 company paid holidays, additional 10 paid days in December when the studio is closed for holiday break, and the ability to accrue weeks of personal paid time-off. Join us and shape the future of an amazing studio culture committed to creating award-winning franchises like Tomb Raider, Marvel's Avengers, the next Perfect Dark, and more! Position Summary Crystal Dynamics is seeking a Senior Character Artist for our growing team! In this role you will partner closely with creative leadership including Art Directors, Lead Artists, and Designers to create quality high-fidelity worlds for future AAA experiences. As a Senior Character Artist you are a highly collaborative and self-motivated professional, with demonstrated excellence in character modeling, texturing, world building, and lighting. As a strong individual contributor, you may mentor junior artists and assist in advancing our creative teams to new heights. Essential Duties & Responsibilities Work in tandem with our Designers, a joined creative and technical effort to build worlds from initial concept to complete, polished form Responsible for the look and artistic integrity of the game Employ cutting edge 3D graphics techniques including: Working with physics-based spaces, high poly modeling (Maya and ZBrush). Baking maps from high-poly to game-res meshes, PBR shader creation and manipulation, and assembly techniques. Additionally, a senior must demonstrate strong visual composition skills to ensure the game’s presentation is of high value artistically With strong drawing, texturing, and lighting skills and an excellent sense of spatial relations and structural logic, will be responsible for creating visually stunning, atmospheric, effective, and efficient natural and realistic characters Conceptualize, present, and execute ideas within a schedule, while actively participating in technical and artistic problem solving Use custom toolsets and deliver assets within the confines of real-time engine constraints and programming demands Work independently and in tandem with Designers to complete assigned projects Optimization of mesh and materials for real-time rendered characters Essential Requirements Experience as Senior Characters Artist (or strong mid who experience taking on senior responsibilities), having contributed to 1+ AAA title (or comparable high-fidelity title) Familiarity with 3D modeling programs such as Maya, 3DS Max, Zbrush, Photoshop, and Substance Painter Experience with physically based rendered art pipelines and related tools (Houdini, Quixel, Substance, etc.) A portfolio demonstrating: Current-gen 3D art techniques and an eye for high-fidelity, realistic, work Ability to create realistic 3D character models and materials, including clothing, armor and other types of hard-surface objects Examples of UV layouts, textures, and bake work Bonus Skills & Experience Traditional art background Familiarity with photogrammetry software Crystal Dynamics is an equal opportunity employer.
