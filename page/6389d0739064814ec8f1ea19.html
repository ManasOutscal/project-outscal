Job Description

RESPONSIBILITIES: Primary responsibility of creating Art and UI designs Actively analyse other top-performing, innovative games and genres in the marketplace to identify and understand trends and behaviours with other developers and audiences and then apply that insight to our product development. Create designs that consider and maximize the effectiveness of typography, icons, grid, and colour systems to layout screens for best results. Apply principles of Visual and Graphic Design (contrast, alignment, repetition, and proximity) to increase ease of use. Approach deliverables, for example, UI Kits, with a systems-oriented mindset that is organized, easy to use by all, and extensible for the future. REQUIRED: 6+ years’ experience in UI & Art Design. Online portfolio Should have strong knowledge in art fundamentals and able to create Icons, UI backgrounds, Logos, UI Interface, buttons, cards etc. Should be a strong visualiser, ability to understand design and business requirement and come up with appealing designs. Translate business requirements, user needs, technical requirements into designs that are visually enticing, easy to use, and emotionally engaging. Should be good in animation fundamentals and willing to learn and deliver UI animations in Unity Should have worked in a team of UI and UX designers. Should be able to operate independently with minimal supervision and check-in. Expected to evangelize the UX methodologies across the production pipeline, evaluate and establish best practices. Excellent written and verbal communication, articulation. Excellent presentation skills Real passion for gaming Bachelor’s degree in graphic arts, interaction design, or equivalent industry experience in design discipline. WHAT WE OFFER YOU: Work in a studio that has complete P&L ownership of games Competitive salary, discretionary annual bonus scheme Full medical, accident as well as life insurance benefits Catered breakfast, lunch and evening snacks Child care facilities for women employees and discounted facilities for male employees Well stocked pantry Generous Paid Maternity/Paternity leave Employee Assistance Programs Active Employee Resource Groups – Women at Zynga Frequent employee events Flexible working hours on many teams Casual dress every single day Work with cool people and impact millions of daily players! HIRING UPDATE: The safety of our candidates and team members is our top priority. During the COVID-19 pandemic, our workforce transitioned to working from home, with all interviewing and onboarding being conducted virtually until further notice.
