Job Description

Hi-Rez Studios is looking for a games-loving UI Designer to work at our studio on SMITE. As a member of our Titan Forge Game Studio, you will join the design team to create top-notch UI for SMITE. You know how to get things done in a collaborative work environment, are super proactive, and you're ready to help us launch our game! Come join the creator of the critically acclaimed SMITE, Paladins, and Rogue Company as we embark on exciting new online game projects! What our UI Designers do at Hi-Rez Studios: Guide the visual and interactive evolution of the product across multiple platforms. Translate complex ideas into elegant, intuitive designs. Assist with requirements gathering and functional specification creation.  Participate in planning, estimating and scheduling activities.  Maintain and grow our library of standard graphical elements. Analyze current screens for UI improvement opportunities. Keep up to date on current design practices and share with the team. Mentor and share knowledge with other technical and non-technical staff. What we are looking for: Extensive experience designing beautiful, engaging user experiences for console and mobile. Bachelor's Degree (or equivalent experience) and 2+ years of experience creating UI and UX for games. Familiarity with usability and HCI principles. Ability to visualize, own, and execute the creation of an experience from initial concept to collaborating with engineers on implementation. Ability to create process flow diagrams, wireframes, and final mockups. Ability to deliver comprehensive designs on a tight deadline. Experience with motion graphics and interface animation principles. Proficiency with the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Adobe XD in particular, Flash and Scaleform). Experience with UE3 Expert eye for detail and consistency; no detail is too small or insignificant. Self-motivated with the ability to collaborate with other team members. Excellent collaborative & communication skills. Video game experience, even if portfolio work
